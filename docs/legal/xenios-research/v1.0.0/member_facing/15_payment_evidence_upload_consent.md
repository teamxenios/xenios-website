## Plain-Language Summary

This consent allows a member to upload optional redacted proof to support manual payment review. The upload does not prove settlement or activate membership.

## Purpose

Payment evidence is collected to help Xenios match a reported payment to an obligation and receiving destination.

## Optional Status

Unless Xenios specifically requests evidence for a disputed or unmatched submission, uploading a screenshot is optional. You may instead provide the transaction reference and other requested details.

## Redaction

Before upload, conceal unrelated balances, unrelated transactions, complete account numbers, and unnecessary personal information. Leave visible only the information needed to match the payment.

## Accepted Files

Accepted formats are JPG, JPEG, PNG, or HEIC image files up to 10 MB per file. Executable files, altered evidence, and unrelated documents are prohibited.

## Access and Storage

Evidence is stored privately and may be viewed only by authorized personnel and service providers for payment review, fraud prevention, accounting, disputes, and legal compliance. Views are logged where supported.

## Retention and Deletion

Evidence is retained for 30 calendar days after final reconciliation unless a longer accounting, dispute, fraud, or legal hold applies, then securely deleted under the records schedule.

## No Automatic Approval

Evidence is supporting information only. Payment is verified only after Xenios confirms receipt in the designated account.

## Accuracy

You certify that evidence is genuine, unaltered except for permitted redaction, and connected to the reported transaction. Fabricated or misleading evidence may result in rejection, suspension, termination, and lawful remedies.
