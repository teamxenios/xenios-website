## Plain-Language Summary

This agreement protects genuinely private Xenios information and member privacy. It does not prohibit honest consumer reviews, protected reporting, legal advice, or legally required disclosures.

## Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes nonpublic portal content, member lists, identities of other members, private payment instructions, supplier and fulfillment information, nonpublic research materials, internal protocols, security information, access credentials, unreleased business plans, proprietary documents, and information clearly marked or reasonably understood as confidential.

## Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that is publicly available without breach, lawfully known without restriction, independently developed, received lawfully from a third party, or an honest consumer review protected by applicable law that does not reveal trade secrets, private personal information, credentials, or other lawfully protected information.

## Member Duties

You will use Confidential Information only for authorized membership purposes, protect it with reasonable care, avoid unauthorized copying or distribution, not share credentials, and promptly report suspected disclosure.

## Member Privacy

Xenios will use reasonable safeguards to protect member identity, ID documents, payment evidence, and private communications and will limit access to persons with a legitimate need.

## Permitted Disclosures

A party may disclose Confidential Information to legal counsel, tax and accounting advisers, insurers, and contractors who are bound by confidentiality, and may disclose as required by law, subpoena, court order, regulator, law enforcement, or protected reporting right. When lawful, the disclosing party should provide notice and limit disclosure to what is required.

## Consumer Reviews and Complaints

Nothing prohibits an honest review, good-faith complaint, or truthful statement about Xenios goods, services, or conduct. A review must not disclose another person’s private information, access credentials, trade secrets, or unlawful content.

## Return and Deletion

Upon request or termination, you must stop using and reasonably delete Xenios Confidential Information, except records lawfully retained for legal advice, proof of rights, or required compliance.

## Remedies

Unauthorized disclosure may cause harm that is difficult to measure. Subject to applicable law, the injured party may seek actual damages and narrowly tailored temporary or injunctive relief. No predetermined penalty applies unless separately versioned and approved by counsel as a lawful estimate rather than a penalty.

## Duration

Trade-secret obligations continue while information remains a trade secret. Other confidentiality obligations continue for 5 years after disclosure or termination, subject to applicable law.
