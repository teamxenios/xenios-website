## Plain-Language Summary

This is the core membership contract. The $50 activation payment includes the first 30 days. Later 30-day periods cost $25 unless pricing is lawfully changed with notice.

## Parties and Membership

This Agreement is between you and Xenios Research doing business as Xenios Research. Membership is private, personal, revocable, nontransferable, and subject to application, identity review, agreement acceptance, payment verification, and continued compliance.

## Eligibility and Approval

You represent that you are at least 21 years of age, have legal capacity to contract, provided accurate information, and are not acting for a prohibited person or purpose. Xenios may approve, deny, suspend, or terminate access in its reasonable discretion, subject to applicable law and any stated refund obligation.

## Founding Membership Pricing

The activation payment is $50. It includes activation and the first 30 calendar days of membership. No additional $25 payment is due at activation. The first later membership payment is $25 and becomes due 30 calendar days after activation. Each verified $25 payment covers the next 30-calendar-day period.

## Activation Date

Membership begins only when Xenios verifies the activation payment and records the membership as active. A screenshot, pending transfer, payment-app message, or member report does not begin the membership period.

## Manual and Future Billing

During the initial phase, members initiate each payment using an available external method and Xenios verifies receipt. No automatic debit is authorized by this Agreement. Xenios may later offer approved recurring billing only after providing required disclosures and obtaining a separate authorization.

## Renewal, Grace Period, and Suspension

Xenios will provide reasonable renewal notices. If a renewal is not verified by the due date, the account may enter a 5-calendar-day grace period and then be suspended. Suspension may block purchasing and private content. Reinstatement may require payment of the current obligation and completion of updated agreements.

## Account Security

You are responsible for safeguarding credentials and promptly reporting unauthorized access. You may not share, sell, transfer, or permit another person to use your account.

## Private Access and Conduct

Access is a limited license, not ownership. You must comply with the confidentiality, acceptable-use, research-use, payment, privacy, and product terms. You may not scrape, reverse engineer, disrupt, bypass controls, impersonate another person, or use the platform unlawfully.

## Separate Product Transactions

Membership does not guarantee product availability, pricing, shipping eligibility, or order acceptance. Each product order is a separate transaction governed by the Product Purchase Terms and lot-specific information.

## Cancellation and Refunds

You may cancel using the cancellation control in the member portal or written notice to samuel@xeniostechnology.com. Cancellation and refunds are governed by the published Cancellation and Refund Policy. Unless that policy or applicable law provides otherwise, cancellation stops future periods but does not retroactively undo a period already activated.

## Changes

Xenios may update operational terms prospectively. Material changes will be disclosed and may require reacceptance. Pricing changes apply only after required notice and do not authorize an automatic charge without separate consent.

## Disputes and Incorporated Terms

The standalone Individual Arbitration, Class-Action Waiver and Jury Waiver is incorporated only if separately accepted. The Privacy Policy, Electronic Consent, Confidentiality Agreement, Research Use Agreement, Manual Payment Terms, Renewal Policy, and applicable product policies are incorporated by reference.

## Termination

Xenios may terminate for material breach, misuse, fraud, false identity information, prohibited product use, payment reversal, confidentiality breach, security risk, legal requirement, or repeated policy violations. Provisions intended by their nature to survive will survive.
