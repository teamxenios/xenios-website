## Plain-Language Summary

This consent lets Xenios provide agreements, notices, receipts, policies, and other records electronically. It explains the technology needed, how to request paper copies, and how to withdraw electronic consent.

## Scope of Consent

You consent to receive, review, sign, and retain electronically all records connected with your application, account, membership, identity review, payments, renewals, product orders, customer support, disputes, and relationship with Xenios Research doing business as Xenios Research ("Xenios"). This consent applies to current and future records until withdrawn.

## Records Covered

Covered records include applications, disclosures, identity consents, membership agreements, privacy notices, payment instructions, receipts, renewal notices, cancellation notices, product terms, shipping policies, amendments, dispute notices, and legally required communications.

## Paper Copies

You may request a paper copy by contacting samuel@xeniostechnology.com. State whether paper copies are free or subject to a reasonable disclosed fee: One paper copy of a signed member agreement is available without charge; reasonable duplication or delivery costs may apply to additional copies after advance disclosure.. A request for a paper copy does not itself withdraw electronic consent.

## Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw consent by following the process at the account settings page in the member portal or contacting samuel@xeniostechnology.com. Withdrawal is prospective and does not affect records already provided or signed. If Xenios cannot lawfully or operationally continue the relationship without electronic delivery, withdrawal may result in suspension or termination after notice.

## Hardware and Software Requirements

You must have a current web browser, internet access, an active email address, software capable of opening HTML and PDF files, and sufficient storage or printing capability. By opening this document and selecting the acceptance box, you demonstrate that you can access and retain the electronic format used by Xenios.

## Contact Information

You must keep your email address and contact details current. Notices sent to the most recent information in your account are treated as delivered to the extent permitted by law.

## Electronic Signature Attribution

Your authenticated account activity, typed legal name, affirmative checkbox, signature event, timestamp, IP address, user agent, and document hash may be used to establish attribution and intent to sign.

## Retention and Reproduction

Xenios will maintain signed records in a form designed to accurately reflect the signed content and remain capable of later access, download, printing, and reproduction for the applicable retention period.

## Changes to Technical Requirements

If a material technology change creates a meaningful risk that you cannot access future electronic records, Xenios will provide revised requirements and any legally required opportunity to withdraw consent.
