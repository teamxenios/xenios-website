## Plain-Language Summary

These terms make each approved product order a separate contract, subject to research-use restrictions, order review, inventory, lot documentation, shipping, and product-specific policies.

## Order Offer and Acceptance

Your order is an offer to purchase. Xenios may review, decline, limit, or cancel before acceptance for inventory, eligibility, compliance, pricing error, payment, shipping, fraud, provider, or legal reasons. Acceptance occurs when Xenios records the order as accepted or submits it for fulfillment.

## Eligibility and Research Use

You must remain an eligible member and comply with the Research Use Agreement. Product availability does not represent approval for human or veterinary use.

## Product Information

Product pages may include identity, amount, lot, COA, storage, handling, and research-use information. Lot-specific records control where they differ from general descriptions. Images may be illustrative.

## Price and Taxes

The displayed price excludes applicable tax, shipping, fees, or duties unless stated. Xenios may correct obvious errors before acceptance and will provide the option to confirm or cancel when required.

## Inventory and Substitution

Products are subject to availability. Xenios will not substitute a materially different compound without affirmative approval.

## Payment

Payment must be completed through an enabled method approved for product purchases. The temporary membership-payment bridge does not automatically authorize product purchases.

## Shipping and Delivery

Shipping is limited to approved United States jurisdictions shown as eligible at checkout; no international shipping unless Xenios expressly authorizes it in writing. You are responsible for a lawful, accurate, secure delivery address. Risk-of-loss and title language is as stated in the final approved agreement and applicable law.

## Inspection and Claims

Inspect the package promptly. Report damage, missing items, wrong products, seal concerns, or lot discrepancies within 72 hours after confirmed delivery for visible damage, shortage, or incorrect items, and 7 calendar days after the expected delivery date for a non-delivery claim with reasonably requested evidence.

## Returns

Because of chain-of-custody and integrity concerns, returned research materials may be ineligible for resale. Return, refund, replacement, and disposal rules appear in the Shipping, Claims and Replacement Policy.

## No Unauthorized Resale

Products may not be resold, redistributed, relabeled, repackaged, exported, or transferred without written authorization and legal compliance.

## Recall and Trace

You will cooperate with a lawful recall, lot trace, safety notice, carrier investigation, or compliance inquiry and stop use or distribution when instructed.
