## Plain-Language Summary

This consent authorizes Xenios to collect and review a government ID for identity, age, eligibility, and fraud-prevention purposes, with limited access and short raw-image retention.

## Purpose

Xenios requires identity and age review to protect the private membership platform, confirm that applicants meet eligibility requirements, reduce fraud, support compliance, and match the person signing agreements and reporting payments.

## Information Reviewed

Authorized personnel may review your legal name, photograph, date of birth, expiration date, issuing jurisdiction, and a masked portion of the document number. Xenios does not request a Social Security number through this process.

## Permitted Redaction

Unless Xenios gives different lawful instructions, you may conceal your street address, machine-readable barcode, and all but the last four characters of the document number, provided your name, photograph, date of birth, expiration date, issuing jurisdiction, and required masked identifier remain readable.

## Manual Review and Providers

The initial process may be a manual visual review rather than forensic document authentication. Xenios may later use manual Xenios review during the initial release and any later approved identity-verification provider disclosed at collection. If a provider is used, its privacy terms will also apply and Xenios will disclose the provider before collection.

## Storage and Access

The file will be stored in private access-controlled storage. Views by administrators are logged. The file will not be placed in public pages, ordinary email, analytics, or member-visible directories.

## Retention and Deletion

Xenios intends to delete the raw ID file within 7 calendar days after completed review, except during a documented legal, fraud, dispute, security, or compliance hold. Xenios may retain the verification result, reviewer, date, issuing jurisdiction, expiration result, masked identifier, and audit trail.

## Failed or Incomplete Review

Xenios may request a clearer image, additional permitted documentation, or correction of inconsistent information. Refusal or inability to complete review may prevent activation. False or altered documentation may result in rejection, suspension, termination, preservation of evidence, and lawful reporting.

## Withdrawal

You may withdraw this consent before review by contacting samuel@xeniostechnology.com. Withdrawal may require deletion of the pending upload and will prevent membership activation. Withdrawal after completed processing does not necessarily require deletion of records that Xenios must lawfully retain.

## No Public Disclosure

Xenios will not publicly disclose your government ID. Disclosures may occur to authorized service providers, legal advisers, insurers, regulators, courts, or law enforcement when permitted or required by law.
