## Plain-Language Summary

This policy explains shipping eligibility, delivery responsibilities, damaged or missing package claims, evidence, replacement review, and exclusions.

## Shipping Eligibility

Xenios ships only to approved locations and may require address, identity, and eligibility matching. International shipping is prohibited unless separately approved.

## Address Accuracy

You are responsible for providing a complete lawful address and promptly correcting errors before fulfillment. Address changes after fulfillment may not be possible.

## Carrier Delays

Estimated delivery dates are not guarantees. Carrier, weather, security, access, or force-majeure delays may occur.

## Delivery and Security

You are responsible for arranging secure receipt, monitoring tracking, and retrieving the package promptly. Signature may be required.

## Inspection

Upon receipt, inspect the outer package, seal, label, vial count, product identity, lot information, and visible condition before opening or handling.

## Claims

Submit a claim within 72 hours after confirmed delivery for visible damage, shortage, or incorrect items, and 7 calendar days after the expected delivery date for a non-delivery claim through the claims section of the authenticated member portal. Include order number, issue description, photographs, packaging, label, lot, carrier information, and other reasonably necessary evidence. Do not submit altered evidence.

## Temperature and Storage

Follow product-specific storage information. A warm package or delayed shipment does not automatically establish loss of integrity. Xenios may review stability, packaging, duration, temperature indicators, and supplier guidance.

## Missing or Stolen Packages

Xenios may require carrier confirmation, delivery-location details, a signed statement, or law-enforcement report where appropriate. Replacement is not guaranteed when tracking confirms delivery to the correct address.

## Remedies

Depending on the facts and law, Xenios may provide information, replacement, store credit, partial refund, full refund, carrier claim, or denial with explanation. A remedy may require return or documented disposal.

## Exclusions

Claims may be denied for late notice, incorrect address, unauthorized forwarding, prohibited storage, member-caused damage, use after discovering a defect, missing evidence, fraud, or violation of product terms, except where law requires relief.
