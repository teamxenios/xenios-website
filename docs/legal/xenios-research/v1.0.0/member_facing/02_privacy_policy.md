## Plain-Language Summary

This policy describes what Xenios collects, why it collects it, how information is protected and shared, how long it is retained, and what privacy choices may apply.

## Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to Xenios Research, a private membership and research-commerce platform operating from Texas. Contact samuel@xeniostechnology.com for privacy questions.

## Information We Collect

We may collect application data, account credentials, contact information, age and identity information, government-ID images, identity-review results, signatures, signed-document records, payment reports, payment evidence, transaction references, membership history, product-order information, shipping information, customer-support communications, device information, IP address, browser data, security logs, and cookie or analytics information.

## Purposes of Processing

We use information to evaluate applications, verify identity and age, prevent fraud, administer membership, provide private portal access, document consent, process and reconcile payments, manage renewals, fulfill approved orders, provide support, enforce agreements, protect members and Xenios, comply with law, and improve security and operations.

## Government Identification

Raw government-ID files are restricted to authorized personnel and service providers with a need to review them. Xenios intends to delete the raw source within 7 calendar days after review unless a documented legal, fraud, dispute, or compliance hold requires a longer period. The verification result and limited audit record may be retained longer.

## Payment Information

During the temporary manual-payment phase, Xenios may collect the payment method, amount, date, sender identifier, external transaction reference, and optional redacted proof. Xenios should not request unrelated balances, complete bank account numbers, or unnecessary transaction history.

## Service Providers and Disclosures

Information may be disclosed to hosting, storage, authentication, email, identity, payment, fulfillment, shipping, analytics, security, legal, accounting, insurance, and compliance providers under appropriate contractual restrictions. Xenios may disclose information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, investigate misuse, complete a transaction requested by you, or in connection with a lawful business transfer.

## No Sale of Government-ID Information

Xenios will not sell raw driver’s-license images or government-ID numbers. Any broader data-sharing or targeted-advertising practice must be described here before use and made subject to applicable opt-out rights.

## Security

Xenios uses administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information, including role-based access, private storage, encryption in transit and at rest where supported, access logging, retention controls, and incident response. No system is completely secure.

## Retention

Information is retained only as reasonably necessary for the purposes described, legal requirements, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute management, and enforcement. The internal retention schedule controls specific periods and legal holds.

## Your Privacy Choices

Subject to applicable law and exceptions, you may request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of certain personal data, appeal a denied request, withdraw certain consent, and opt out of certain processing. Submit requests to samuel@xeniostechnology.com. Xenios may authenticate requests before responding.

## State Privacy Rights

Additional rights may apply based on residency. Xenios will not require a member to waive a privacy right that applicable law makes nonwaivable. State-specific notices should be added before expanding nationally.

## Security Incidents

Xenios will investigate suspected incidents and provide notices required by applicable law. Members should promptly report suspected unauthorized access to samuel@xeniostechnology.com.

## Children

The platform is not intended for anyone under 21 years of age. Xenios does not knowingly approve minors.

## Changes

Material changes will be posted with a new effective date and, when required, presented for affirmative reacceptance.
