## Plain-Language Summary

These terms govern the temporary external payment process. A member report or screenshot is not payment. Xenios activates membership only after an administrator confirms funds were received.

## Temporary External Methods

During a limited bridge period, Xenios may provide one or more authenticated applicants with external payment instructions. Availability may change immediately based on provider terms, security, operational capacity, or law.

## Payment Reference

You must use the exact Xenios payment reference and amount shown in your account. Do not use a misleading memo or conceal the nature of the transaction. Contact samuel@xeniostechnology.com before paying if the recipient information or amount is unclear.

## Payment Completion

Payment is complete only when Xenios confirms that cleared funds were received in the designated destination and marks the obligation verified. A screenshot, pending status, payment-app notification, bank memo, or your submission is supporting evidence only.

## Member Submission

After sending payment, you may report the method, amount, date, approximate time, sender name, masked sender identifier, external reference, and optional redacted evidence. Do not upload unrelated balances, complete account numbers, or unrelated transaction history.

## Review and Timing

Xenios will review submissions in a commercially reasonable period but does not guarantee immediate activation. Xenios may request more information, mark an amount mismatch, detect a duplicate, reject unverifiable evidence, or require a replacement payment method.

## Wrong Recipient and Provider Error

You are responsible for verifying that you are using the current instructions displayed in your authenticated account. Xenios is not responsible for money sent to an unauthorized person, outdated instruction, mistyped address, impostor, or account not designated by Xenios, except to the extent caused by Xenios conduct for which liability cannot lawfully be disclaimed.

## Fees and Reversals

You are responsible for payment-app or bank fees unless stated otherwise. If a payment is reversed, charged back, recalled, disputed, or found fraudulent, Xenios may suspend access, reverse the ledger entry, cancel orders, seek repayment, and exercise contractual remedies.

## Duplicates and Refunds

Report duplicate payments promptly. Approved refunds are returned through a lawful method selected by Xenios after identity and transaction verification. Xenios may offset undisputed amounts owed where permitted by law.

## Bridge Sunset

The temporary bridge is expected to end 14 calendar days after the configured launch date. Xenios may replace temporary instructions with approved business methods and migrate unpaid obligations. Historical records remain preserved.

## No Cash

Cash is not accepted unless Xenios provides a specific written receipt process approved by management and counsel.
