## Plain-Language Summary

The first 30 days are included in the $50 activation payment. Each later 30-day period costs $25. Manual payments require verification until separate recurring authorization is implemented.

## Initial Period

The initial membership period begins when the $50 activation payment is verified and ends 30 calendar days later.

## Renewal Amount and Period

The next payment is $25. Each verified $25 renewal covers the next 30-calendar-day period. Xenios will calculate dates server-side and display them in the account.

## Manual Renewal

During manual billing, you must initiate each renewal payment. Xenios does not have authority under this Policy to debit a card, bank account, or payment app automatically.

## Notices

Xenios intends to send notices approximately seven days and three days before the due date, on the due date, and during any grace period. Failure to receive a courtesy reminder does not automatically cancel an otherwise valid disclosed obligation, subject to applicable law.

## Verification Timing

A renewal is credited when Xenios verifies receipt. If you pay near the deadline, submit the report promptly. Xenios may temporarily preserve access during review under a documented grace policy.

## Grace Period and Suspension

The grace period is 5-calendar-day. At its end, access may be suspended for nonpayment. Suspension does not erase obligations already incurred or provisions that survive termination.

## Reinstatement

Reinstatement may require payment of a current renewal, completion of updated agreements, and resolution of reversals or compliance concerns. Xenios will not charge for suspended periods unless the published policy clearly states otherwise and applicable law permits.

## Cancellation

You may cancel using the cancellation control in the member portal or written notice to samuel@xeniostechnology.com. Cancellation prevents future periods after the effective date but is subject to the Cancellation and Refund Policy.

## Future Automatic Billing

Automatic or recurring billing may be offered only through an approved provider after clear disclosures and separate express authorization. Members who do not authorize automatic billing may be offered manual methods where operationally available.
