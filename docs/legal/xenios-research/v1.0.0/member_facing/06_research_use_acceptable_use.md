## Plain-Language Summary

This agreement restricts platform and product use to the permitted research context and prohibits human use, veterinary use, resale, relabeling, and other misuse.

## Research-Use Representation

You represent that any research product obtained through Xenios will be acquired, handled, stored, and used only for lawful, legitimate research purposes consistent with the product label, published restrictions, and applicable law.

## Prohibited Uses

You will not use or permit any product for human consumption, self-administration, administration to another person, veterinary use, diagnosis, treatment, mitigation, cure, disease prevention, compounding, food use, cosmetic injection, performance enhancement, or any purpose inconsistent with its research-use labeling.

## No Resale or Redistribution

You may not resell, redistribute, relabel, repackage, transfer, export, supply to a clinic or consumer, or make a product available to another person unless Xenios provides prior written authorization and all legal requirements are satisfied.

## No Medical Representations

You will not claim or imply that Xenios, its suppliers, products, documents, staff, or members provide medical advice or that a product is approved, safe, or effective for human or veterinary use.

## Handling and Storage

You are responsible for qualified personnel, facilities, protective equipment, storage, chain of custody, documentation, disposal, and compliance appropriate to the material. Product-specific label, COA, safety, and storage information controls where more restrictive.

## Accurate Information

You will provide accurate identity, organization, research-purpose, shipping, and payment information and will promptly update material changes.

## Order Review

Xenios may request research-purpose information, limit quantities, decline orders, hold fulfillment, cancel transactions, or suspend access when activity appears inconsistent with stated research use, provider restrictions, law, safety, or compliance controls.

## Reporting

You will promptly report suspected diversion, misuse, theft, unauthorized resale, adverse incidents, security compromise, or legally significant product complaint to samuel@xeniostechnology.com.

## Cooperation

You will reasonably cooperate with lawful compliance review, product recall, lot trace, shipping claim, fraud investigation, or regulatory inquiry. This does not require waiver of protected rights.

## Consequences

Violation may result in order cancellation, membership suspension or termination, preservation of records, refusal of future service, indemnification obligations, and lawful reporting.
