## Plain-Language Summary

This supplemental agreement provides the strongest defensible allocation of risk while preserving claims and rights that cannot legally be waived. It is presented as a separate, conspicuous agreement requiring its own affirmative acceptance.

## Conspicuous Notice

PLEASE READ CAREFULLY. THIS AGREEMENT RELEASES CERTAIN CLAIMS, LIMITS CERTAIN REMEDIES, REQUIRES YOU TO ASSUME CERTAIN RISKS, AND REQUIRES INDEMNIFICATION FOR SPECIFIED MEMBER MISCONDUCT. IT DOES NOT RELEASE CLAIMS THAT APPLICABLE LAW DOES NOT PERMIT TO BE RELEASED.

## Voluntary Assumption of Risk

You knowingly and voluntarily assume risks inherent in lawful access to a private research platform, possession and handling of research materials, third-party payment applications, shipping, storage, data transmission, service interruptions, and reliance on information expressly identified as nonmedical and nonprofessional, except to the extent caused by conduct that cannot lawfully be disclaimed.

## Release of Released Claims

To the fullest extent permitted by law, you release Xenios and its owners, officers, employees, affiliates, contractors, suppliers, fulfillment providers, carriers, and agents (the “Released Parties”) from claims arising from ordinary negligence and from risks you expressly assumed, including platform interruption, unauthorized use caused by your credential sharing, third-party provider failure, carrier delay, member misuse, unauthorized storage, and use contrary to product restrictions.

## Excluded Claims

The release does not apply to fraud, intentional misconduct, claims that applicable law makes nonwaivable, or any category counsel determines cannot be released in advance. Gross-negligence treatment must be completed for each applicable jurisdiction.

## Covenant Not to Sue

For a Released Claim, you covenant not to begin or maintain a court action against a Released Party and will instead comply with the separately accepted dispute process. This covenant does not prohibit small-claims matters, provisional relief, protected regulatory reports, lawful subpoenas, emergency assistance, or claims excluded from arbitration or waiver by law.

## Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, aggregate liability for claims not otherwise released will not exceed the greater of one hundred U.S. dollars (US $100) or the total amount paid by the member to Xenios during the six months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim. This cap does not apply where law prohibits limitation. No party is liable for speculative, indirect, special, exemplary, or consequential damages except where such limitation is prohibited or an award is expressly authorized by a nonwaivable law.

## No Reliance

You acknowledge that you are not relying on oral statements, testimonials, private messages, third-party posts, or representations not expressly included in the signed agreement and approved product information.

## Member Indemnification

You will defend, indemnify, and hold the Released Parties harmless from third-party claims, losses, fines, penalties, recalls, chargebacks, costs, and reasonable attorneys’ fees arising from your unauthorized human or veterinary use, misuse, resale, redistribution, relabeling, false representations, unlawful conduct, credential sharing, infringement, fabricated payment evidence, or breach of confidentiality. Indemnification does not apply to the extent a claim was caused by a Released Party’s conduct for which indemnity cannot lawfully be required.

## Mitigation and Cooperation

Each party will reasonably mitigate loss and cooperate in defense. Xenios may control the defense of an indemnified claim, but may not settle a claim imposing personal liability or admission on you without reasonable consent.

## Severability and Narrowing

A court or arbitrator should narrow any overbroad term to the maximum enforceable scope rather than void the entire agreement, where applicable law permits.
