# Xenios Research Legal and Operating Package — Final Release

**Status:** FINAL APPROVED RELEASE  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

This package contains 17 member-facing agreements and notices, 18 internal operating policies, the signing and implementation maps, and the legal approval records supplied by Xenios Research General Counsel.

## Canonical Commercial Terms

- Founding Membership activation: **$50**.
- The $50 activation includes the first **30 calendar days** of membership.
- The first later membership payment is **$25**, due 30 calendar days after verified activation.
- Each later verified $25 payment covers the next 30-calendar-day period.
- During the initial manual bridge, a payment report or screenshot does not activate membership. An authorized Xenios administrator must verify actual receipt.
- Raw government-ID files are scheduled for deletion 7 calendar days after completed identity review unless a documented hold applies.
- Ordinary founding members are not required to enroll in MFA during the initial release. Administrative MFA remains an internal security control.

## Publication Rule

Only the exact effective document version and content hash recorded by the signing system may be presented for acceptance. Agreement text may not be changed silently after publication.
