# Final Member Signing Sequence

1. Electronic Records and Signature Consent
2. Privacy Policy acknowledgment
3. Identity Verification and Government ID Consent
4. Founding Membership Agreement
5. Private Membership Confidentiality and Nondisclosure Agreement
6. Research Use and Acceptable Use Agreement
7. No-Medical-Advice and Assumption-of-Risk Acknowledgment
8. Individual Arbitration, Class-Action Waiver and Jury Waiver — separate conspicuous acceptance
9. Release, Waiver, Covenant Not to Sue, Limitation of Liability and Indemnification Agreement — separate conspicuous acceptance
10. Manual Payment and Verification Terms
11. Membership Renewal Policy
12. Cancellation and Refund Policy
13. Website Terms of Use
14. Payment Evidence Upload Consent — before any evidence upload
15. Product Purchase Terms — at or before first product checkout and again after material revision
16. Shipping, Claims and Replacement Policy — at or before first product checkout and again after material revision
17. Cookie and Tracking Notice — notice and consent where required by deployed cookies

## Activation Gate

Membership may become active only after the required agreements above are accepted, identity review is complete, and the $50 activation payment is verified by an authorized administrator.
