# State-Law Issue Checklist

For each state served, counsel should review:

- Arbitration formation and unconscionability
- Negligence release and exculpatory clause rules
- Gross negligence and willful misconduct exclusions
- Indemnification restrictions
- Limitation-of-liability rules
- Automatic renewal and cancellation requirements
- Privacy notices and consumer rights
- Sensitive-data consent and government-ID rules
- Data-breach notification
- Consumer health data laws
- Sales tax and membership tax
- Research-product restrictions
- Shipping and hazardous-material rules
- Warranty disclaimers and UCC remedies
- Refund and “all sales final” limits
- Age restrictions
- Electronic signature requirements
- Choice of law and forum enforceability
- Small-claims and statutory remedy preservation
