# Defined Terms Glossary

- **Applicant:** A person who submitted an application but is not active.
- **Approved Applicant:** An applicant permitted to complete activation steps.
- **Member:** A person whose membership is active.
- **Xenios:** Xenios Research doing business as Xenios Research.
- **Platform:** The private website, portal, applications, and related systems.
- **Research Product:** A product expressly offered for permitted research use under its label and terms.
- **Confidential Information:** Nonpublic information protected by the Confidentiality Agreement.
- **External Payment Method:** A method completed outside the Xenios checkout and later verified manually.
- **Payment Obligation:** The amount and purpose recorded as due in the member account.
- **Payment Submission:** The member’s report that an external payment was sent.
- **Verified Payment:** Funds confirmed by an authorized administrator in the designated receiving destination.
- **Membership Period:** A 30-calendar-day period activated by a verified payment.
- **Covered Dispute:** A dispute included in the standalone Arbitration Agreement.
- **Released Parties:** The persons and entities identified in the Release and Waiver.
- **Raw ID Source:** The uploaded image of a government identity document.
- **Verification Record:** The limited result retained after the raw ID source is deleted.
