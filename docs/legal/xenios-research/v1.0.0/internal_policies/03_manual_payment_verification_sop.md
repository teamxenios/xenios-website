# Manual Payment Verification SOP

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Purpose

Prevent screenshot-based or duplicate activation.

## Procedure

Match obligation, amount, member reference, sender information, destination, and actual account receipt. Record external reference and settlement date.

## Four-Eyes Rule

For amounts or patterns above US $500 or any amount presenting unusual fraud, reversal, identity, or reconciliation risk, require a second administrator.

## No Autoactivation

Payment evidence alone never changes membership status.

## Exceptions

Amount mismatch, duplicate, reversal, wrong destination, or fraud must enter a documented exception state.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
