# Manual Identity Review SOP

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Purpose

Establish a consistent, minimal-data manual review for approved applicants.

## Roles

Only trained administrators with the identity-review role may access raw ID sources. The reviewer must not be the applicant.

## Procedure

Confirm consent, compare legal name and photograph, confirm age threshold and expiration, record issuing jurisdiction and masked identifier, document outcome, and avoid copying unnecessary data.

## Outcomes

Verified, information requested, rejected, expired, or escalated to counsel/compliance.

## Prohibited

Do not download to personal devices, email files, store copies in chat, or claim forensic authentication.

## Audit

Every view and decision must be logged. Quality review should sample completed cases.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
