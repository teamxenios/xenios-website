# Bank Compliance Response Package Checklist

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Core Records

Entity documents, EIN, ownership, source of funds, financial statements, website, agreements, catalog, invoices, suppliers, and expected transactions.

## Narrative

Explain technology, membership, research products, payment flows, fulfillment, refunds, and controls accurately.

## Monitoring

Respond promptly, preserve records, and escalate closures or freezes to counsel and accounting.

## Prohibited

Do not mislabel the business, hide product categories, or fabricate invoices.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
