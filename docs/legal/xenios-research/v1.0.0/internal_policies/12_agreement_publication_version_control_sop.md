# Agreement Publication and Version-Control SOP

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Drafting

Drafts remain non-signable and labeled.

## Approval

Publication requires completed required fields, counsel status, effective date, hash, signing rule, and authorized publisher.

## Immutability

Never edit a published version in place.

## Reacceptance

Material changes create a required reacceptance campaign.

## Archive

Preserve superseded content and acceptance records.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
