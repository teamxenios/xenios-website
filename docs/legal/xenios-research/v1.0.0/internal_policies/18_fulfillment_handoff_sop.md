# Fulfillment Handoff Procedure

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Eligibility

Only accepted, paid, approved orders with released SKUs and valid shipping data may be handed off.

## Data Minimization

Send the minimum fulfillment data. Do not send identity documents or payment evidence.

## Acknowledgment

Require provider acknowledgment, order reference, lot assignment, tracking, and exception status.

## Events

Handle cancellation, shipment, delivery, damage, return, replacement, and recall idempotently.

## Audit

Record every handoff and provider response.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
