# Refund Approval SOP

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Eligibility

Apply the published policy and nonwaivable law.

## Verification

Confirm original payment, recipient identity, amount, reason, prior refunds, and absence of a conflicting reversal.

## Approval

Refunds above US $250 require two approvers.

## Execution

Use a lawful approved method, record external reference, and generate notice.

## Controls

Never refund to a different unverified person solely because a member requests it.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
