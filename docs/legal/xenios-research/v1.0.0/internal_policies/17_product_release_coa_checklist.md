# Product Release and COA Checklist

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Required

Exact identity, supplier, lot, COA, test methods, label, amount, storage, shipping, pricing, claims, restrictions, inventory, and fulfillment mapping.

## Approval

Science/compliance, operations, legal, and administrator release.

## No Release

No SKU goes live with missing real COA, misleading claims, mismatched lot, or unapproved payment/fulfillment route.

## Change Control

New lot, supplier, label, or material claim requires review.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
