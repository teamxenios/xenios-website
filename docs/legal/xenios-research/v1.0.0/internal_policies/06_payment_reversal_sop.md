# Payment Reversal SOP

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Trigger

Bank recall, app reversal, chargeback, fraud report, returned ACH, or administrative correction.

## Response

Freeze affected obligation, preserve evidence, notify member, reverse ledger only through append-only correction, and evaluate membership suspension.

## Orders

Hold unfulfilled orders and coordinate with fulfillment for shipped orders.

## Resolution

Document repayment, successful dispute, write-off, or termination.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
