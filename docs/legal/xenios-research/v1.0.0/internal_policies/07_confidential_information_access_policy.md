# Confidential Information Access Policy

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Principle

Least privilege and need-to-know access.

## Access Tiers

Separate roles for identity, payment, legal, support, fulfillment, and system administration.

## Prohibited

No credential sharing, bulk export without approval, personal-device storage, or use for unrelated purposes.

## Monitoring

Log sensitive views, exports, changes, and deletions.

## Termination

Remove access promptly when role or employment changes.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
