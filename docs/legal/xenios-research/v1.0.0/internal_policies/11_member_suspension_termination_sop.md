# Member Suspension and Termination SOP

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Grounds

Nonpayment, reversal, security risk, false identity, misuse, prohibited product conduct, confidentiality breach, or legal requirement.

## Process

Record grounds, evidence, authority, member notice, access changes, pending orders, and appeal or cure opportunity where appropriate.

## Emergency

Immediate temporary suspension is allowed for security, safety, fraud, or legal risk, followed by prompt review.

## Data

Termination does not mean immediate deletion of records required for law, accounting, disputes, or security.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
