# Day 15 Payment Migration SOP

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Objective

End temporary personal receiving methods and move new obligations to approved business methods.

## Pre-Cutover

Confirm entity accounts, provider permission, test payment, refund test, reconciliation, terms, instructions, and support.

## Cutover

Disable temporary methods for new obligations, preserve pending reviews, migrate unpaid obligations, notify affected members, and monitor failures.

## Accounting

Reconcile bridge receipts and document lawful transfers and classifications with accounting.

## No Concealment

Do not split, rename, or route transactions to evade provider or bank review.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
