# Incident and Data-Breach Response Plan

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Preparation

Maintain contacts, system inventory, legal matrix, vendors, backups, and communication templates.

## Response

Identify, contain, preserve evidence, eradicate, recover, and document.

## Assessment

Determine data types, people affected, acquisition risk, jurisdictions, vendors, and notification duties.

## Notification

Counsel directs legally required notices to individuals, regulators, providers, and insurers.

## Post-Incident

Complete root cause, control improvements, and board or executive report.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
