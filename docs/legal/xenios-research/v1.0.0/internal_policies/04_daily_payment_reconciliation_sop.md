# Daily Payment Reconciliation SOP

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Inputs

Payment obligations, member submissions, receiving-account activity, refunds, reversals, and ledger entries.

## Daily Steps

Reconcile totals by destination and method, investigate unmatched transactions, confirm no duplicate ledger entry, and sign the daily report.

## Escalation

Escalate unexplained differences, unusual volume, provider warnings, or suspected fraud.

## Retention

Retain reports according to accounting and dispute requirements without storing unnecessary personal financial details.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
