# Driver’s License Retention and Deletion SOP

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Policy

Raw ID sources are deleted within the approved short retention period after review unless a documented hold applies.

## Automatic Deletion

A scheduled job identifies eligible files, verifies no hold, deletes the object, records the storage response, and marks the deletion timestamp.

## Legal Holds

Holds require purpose, approver, start date, review date, and expiration or release.

## Verification

A second system check confirms the object is inaccessible and no public or cached copy remains.

## Records

Retain only the limited verification record and audit trail.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
