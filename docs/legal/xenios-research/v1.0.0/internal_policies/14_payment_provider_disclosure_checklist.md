# Payment Provider Disclosure Package Checklist

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Business

Entity, owners, website, business model, membership, product categories, expected volume, fulfillment, and customer profile.

## Compliance

Catalog, labels, COAs, terms, identity controls, research-use controls, refund policy, shipping, chargeback controls, and support.

## Technical

Checkout flow, descriptor, webhooks, fraud tools, data security, and settlement accounts.

## Attestation

Disclosures must be accurate and updated when the catalog or volume changes.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
