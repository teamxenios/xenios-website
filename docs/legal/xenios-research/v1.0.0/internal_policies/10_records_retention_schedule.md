# Records Retention Schedule

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Principle

Keep records only as long as needed for purpose, law, accounting, disputes, fraud, and enforcement.

## Categories

Applications, identity sources, identity results, agreements, payment evidence, ledger, tax records, orders, support, security logs, and incidents.

## Schedule

Counsel and accounting must set a period for each category. Raw ID sources and payment screenshots should have shorter periods than signed agreements and ledgers.

## Holds

Legal holds suspend ordinary deletion for specified records.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
