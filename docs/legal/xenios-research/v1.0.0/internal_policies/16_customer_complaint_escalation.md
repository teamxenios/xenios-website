# Customer Complaint and Escalation Procedure

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Intake

Record member, issue, order, dates, requested resolution, safety concerns, and documents.

## Triage

Separate payment, privacy, identity, product, shipping, safety, legal, and accessibility issues.

## Escalation

Immediately escalate threats, adverse incidents, regulator contacts, data incidents, fraud, and legal demands.

## Resolution

Provide a documented response and preserve the right to give honest reviews or make protected reports.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
