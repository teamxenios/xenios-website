# Administrator Access and MFA Policy

**Status:** APPROVED FOR INTERNAL OPERATIONAL USE  
**Document ID:** Supporting Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Prepared for:** Xenios Research  
**Effective date:** July 22, 2026  

## Plain-Language Summary

This internal policy governs Xenios operations. It is not a member-facing agreement and must be followed by assigned personnel, subject to documented exceptions and change control.

## Requirement

Administrators with identity, payment, agreement publication, or secret-management access must enroll MFA before production access.

## Sessions

Use short sensitive-action reauthentication and device/session review.

## Recovery

Recovery requires documented identity verification and two-person approval where feasible.

## Monitoring

Alert on new devices, repeated failures, role changes, and suspicious exports.

## Required Records

- Process owner
- Effective date and version
- Training record
- Exceptions and approvals
- Audit evidence
- Review date
