## 1. What this document covers

These terms explain Xenios 90: the three-month roadmap included in your active membership. "Xenios" means [ENTITY], operating the Xenios Research Founding Membership. Xenios 90 sits under your Whole-Life Blueprint (XR-MEM-014) and above your monthly Xenios 30 plans (XR-MEM-015).

## 2. What Xenios 90 is

Xenios 90 is your direction over a three-month arc. Where Xenios 30 answers "what do I do this month", Xenios 90 answers "where are these months taking me". The roadmap is organized in three named phases:

```text
Month 1: Foundation
Month 2: Progression
Month 3: Consolidation
```

The phase names describe the intent of each month:

- Foundation: establish the starting structure. The first month is about putting the plan's core pieces in place and building the routine the later months depend on.
- Progression: build on what held. The second month adjusts and advances what the Foundation month established, informed by your tracker data and check-in.
- Consolidation: make it stick. The third month is about stabilizing gains and turning the working parts of the plan into durable habits, setting up the next cycle.

What each phase contains for you specifically comes from your assessment, your Blueprint, and your monthly check-ins. The phase names are structure, not promises.

## 3. Included in membership

- Xenios 90 is included for every active member. There is no separate charge for it.
- Every month within the roadmap receives new documents: the month's Xenios 30, fitness PDF, and nutrition PDF, published as separate secure versioned PDFs in the Document Center.
- The roadmap does not obligate you to purchase anything. Product guidance is guidance; purchases are always your separate decision.

## 4. How the roadmap and the monthly plan work together

1. The reviewed Blueprint sets your goals and priorities.
2. Xenios 90 lays those priorities across three months.
3. Each month's Xenios 30 turns the current phase into an exact plan.
4. The monthly Review Week (XR-MEM-019) checks progress and produces the next month's documents, keeping the roadmap and the monthly plan aligned.

During the founding phase, Samuel personally reviews every initial and monthly plan package, under the 48 elapsed hour review commitment described in XR-MEM-014. That commitment is a monitored service standard, not a contractual guarantee.

## 5. A roadmap may stay the course

Like the monthly plan, the roadmap is not changed for the sake of change. When the current direction is the best decision, a monthly refresh may leave the roadmap materially unchanged, and a phase may be extended or repeated where that serves your goals better than moving on. An unchanged roadmap is a deliberate decision, recorded as a new versioned document for the month.

## 6. Your part

The roadmap depends on your disclosures and your logging. Keep your allergies, restrictions, health context, and goals current, and complete your monthly check-in during Review Week so each phase is built on real information. Xenios relies on what you disclose and does not independently verify it.

## 7. Limits and safety

1. Xenios 90 is not medical care, diagnosis, treatment, or a prescription, and it contains no medication direction or dosing instructions.
2. The fitness components are governed by the Fitness Program Acknowledgment (XR-MEM-017) and the nutrition components by the Nutrition Program Acknowledgment (XR-MEM-018), including their stop-if-symptoms guidance.
3. No outcome is guaranteed at any phase. Phase names (Foundation, Progression, Consolidation) describe intent and sequence, not promised results.
4. Any research product referenced in the roadmap remains a research product whose classification and permitted marketing lane are under formal review. Nothing in the roadmap changes a product's legal status.
5. Emergencies go to emergency services (911 in the US).

These terms do not waive rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, and they do not relieve Xenios of duties imposed by law.

## 8. Cancellation

If you cancel your membership, access ends immediately, including access to the roadmap and its monthly documents in the Document Center, and remaining paid time is forfeited, with no prorated refund unless applicable law requires one. This policy is subject to applicable law and counsel review. Download the documents you want to keep before you confirm cancellation. Cancellation does not erase records Xenios must keep, and your privacy rights survive cancellation.
