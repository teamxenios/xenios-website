## 1. Purpose

Xenios Research uses artificial intelligence (AI) tools in building your plans and answering
your questions. This disclosure explains where AI is used, where a human is in charge, and
what you can always ask for. Xenios prefers that you know exactly how your plan is made.

## 2. Where AI is used

1. Drafting plans. AI tools help organize your assessment answers and draft the Blueprint,
   Xenios 30 plans, Xenios 90 roadmap, and the fitness and nutrition PDFs.
2. Drafting answers. AI tools may help draft responses to questions you send through
   Telegram or the member site.
3. Organizing data. AI tools may help summarize tracker entries, voice notes, and check-in
   information so review is faster and more consistent.

## 3. Where the human is

1. A named human reviews and publishes. During the founding phase, Samuel Boadu personally
   reviews every initial and monthly plan before it is published to you. The review
   commitment is 48 elapsed hours, including weekends. A plan reaches you only after that
   review.
2. Accountability is personal, not systemic. The accountable human for escalations is the
   founder (samuel@xeniostechnology.com). Responsibility for what is published is never
   attributed to "the system".
3. If the founding-phase review model changes (for example additional reviewers), this
   disclosure will be updated and the change communicated before it takes effect.

## 4. What AI never does here

1. No automated diagnosis. No AI feature diagnoses any condition.
2. No automated prescription. No AI feature prescribes, doses, or directs medication, and
   no plan is a prescription (see XR-MEM-009).
3. No automated final decision on your plan. During the founding phase, no plan is published
   to you by AI alone.
4. No hidden AI identity. Where you interact with an AI-assisted channel, that assistance is
   disclosed. AI output is not presented as if a human wrote it unaided.

## 5. Your right to human review

1. You can always request human review. At any time, for any plan, answer, or
   recommendation, you may ask that a human review it. Send the request through Telegram or
   the member site; it routes to Samuel during the founding phase.
2. No penalty. Requesting human review costs nothing and does not affect your membership,
   pricing, or standing.
3. Errors happen. AI-assisted drafting can produce mistakes. If anything in a plan or answer
   looks wrong for your situation, do not follow it; flag it and ask for human review. Also
   remember one free early plan-change request is available each month.

## 6. What this disclosure does not change

This disclosure does not waive rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, and it does
not relieve Xenios of duties imposed by law. It does not change the educational nature of
the materials (XR-MEM-009), the absence of outcome promises (XR-MEM-007), or how your health
data is handled (XR-MEM-012).
