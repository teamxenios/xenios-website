## 1. What this covenant is

Xenios Research is a private membership. The materials inside it are made for approved members and are not published to the public. This covenant is your agreement to keep those materials inside the membership. It is part of the Founding Membership Agreement (XR-MEM-001) and works alongside the conduct rules in the Membership Covenant (XR-MEM-005). "You" is the member; "we" is [ENTITY], operating as Xenios.

This covenant protects materials. It is deliberately written not to restrict your own voice, your own story, or your legal rights. Section 4 and section 5 come first in any conflict.

## 2. What you agree to keep confidential

"Member materials" means the non-public materials made available to you through the membership, including:

- the member-only Guides,
- your Blueprint, Xenios 30 plans, Xenios 90 roadmaps, and the monthly fitness and nutrition PDFs, as document formats and content authored by Xenios,
- member-only product pages, member pricing, and catalog information not shown to the public, and
- any material Xenios marks as member-only or confidential.

You agree not to republish, repost, resell, mass-share, or otherwise distribute member materials outside the membership. Do not upload them to public sites, share archives of them, or provide them to non-members as a substitute for membership.

## 3. What is not confidential

None of the following is restricted by this covenant:

- your own personal information and data, including your own assessment answers, tracker entries, progress media, and results; they are yours,
- your own experience, opinions, and story as a member, told in your own words,
- information that is publicly available without a breach of this covenant, and
- general knowledge and ideas. Discussing concepts you learned, in your own words, is fine. Copying our documents out to the public internet is not.

## 4. Carve-outs that always apply

Nothing in this covenant restricts you from:

- disclosing materials when law requires it, including under a subpoena, court order, or other legal process,
- reporting suspected wrongdoing, safety concerns, product concerns, or adverse events to any regulator, law-enforcement agency, or public-safety authority, including the FDA and FTC,
- consulting your own lawyer, doctor, or other professional adviser about anything, including member materials; show your doctor anything you want, or
- exercising any right that law protects and that cannot be waived by contract.

These carve-outs need no permission from Xenios and no advance notice to Xenios.

## 5. This is not a gag clause

This covenant does not restrict reviews or protected speech:

- You may post truthful reviews and assessments of Xenios, the membership, and your experience, positive or negative, on any platform. This covenant is designed to comply with the Consumer Review Fairness Act, which makes review-gagging contract terms void, and we will never invoke this covenant against a truthful review.
- You may speak about pricing you paid and service you received.
- We will never ask you to take down truthful statements about your own experience as a condition of membership, refund, or anything else.

## 6. How long it lasts

Your confidentiality obligations for member materials you received continue after your membership ends, whether by cancellation (XR-MEM-004) or termination (XR-MEM-001 section 16). The carve-outs in sections 3, 4, and 5 also continue and always control.
