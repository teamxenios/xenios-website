## 1. Purpose

This acknowledgment records that you understand the kinds of risks that come with the
activities the Xenios Research membership supports, and that you choose to participate with
that understanding. It is presented before payment so you can decline before spending money.

## 2. Activities this acknowledgment covers

- following a fitness program, including the monthly fitness PDF and any exercise it describes,
- changing your diet, including following the monthly nutrition PDF,
- taking dietary supplements purchased through or outside Xenios,
- purchasing or handling research products offered through the member catalog, whose
  classification and permitted marketing lane are under formal review,
- logging tracker data, including progress photos and exercise videos, where physical
  activity is involved in creating them.

## 3. Risks you should understand

1. Physical activity carries risk. Exercise can cause muscle strain, joint injury, falls,
   and in some people serious cardiovascular events. Risk rises with pre-existing conditions,
   deconditioning, poor technique, and overexertion.
2. Dietary change carries risk. Changing what or how much you eat can interact with medical
   conditions, medications, allergies, and intolerances. Rapid or extreme change carries more
   risk than gradual change.
3. Supplements carry risk. Supplements can interact with medications and conditions, and
   product quality varies across the market. No supplement is represented as safe for
   everyone.
4. Research products carry risk and regulatory uncertainty. Their classification is under
   formal review, and Xenios does not provide dosing instructions, administration
   instructions, or medication direction for them.
5. Not every risk can be listed. This section describes categories, not a complete list.

## 4. Your responsibilities

1. Consult your own physician or qualified health professional before starting or changing
   an exercise program, diet, or supplement routine, and before any decision that involves a
   medical condition or medication. Xenios does not provide medical care and is not a
   substitute for your own professionals.
2. Answer the mandatory assessment accurately, including allergies, restrictions, and basic
   safety context, so plans can account for what you disclose.
3. Stop and seek care when something is wrong. Stop any activity that causes chest pain,
   severe shortness of breath, faintness, sharp pain, or any symptom that concerns you, and
   seek medical attention.
4. Emergencies go to emergency services. Call 911 in the United States. The membership,
   the tracker, and Telegram support are not an emergency service and are not monitored as one
   (normal Telegram response target is approximately 12 hours).

## 5. What this acknowledgment does not do

1. This acknowledgment does not waive rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, and
   it does not relieve Xenios of duties imposed by law.
2. It is not a release of liability for conduct the law does not allow to be released, such as
   gross negligence or willful misconduct where applicable law so provides.
3. It does not make any Xenios plan, Guide, product, or answer medical advice, and it does not
   transfer Xenios's own legal obligations (product quality, truthful claims, privacy,
   security) to you.

## 6. Your acknowledgment

By accepting, you confirm each of the following:

1. I understand the categories of risk described above, including risks of physical activity,
   dietary change, supplements, and research products.
2. I choose to participate voluntarily, and I am 21 or older and verified as such.
3. I will consult my own physician or qualified professionals before acting on plans or
   materials, especially where a medical condition or medication is involved.
4. I will provide accurate assessment information and update it when it changes.
5. I understand emergencies go to emergency services (911 in the US), not to Xenios.
6. I understand this acknowledgment does not take away rights the law gives me and does not
   remove Xenios's legal duties.
