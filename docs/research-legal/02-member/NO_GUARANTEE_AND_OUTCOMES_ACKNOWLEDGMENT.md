## 1. Purpose

This acknowledgment records that you, the approved applicant activating a Xenios Research
Founding Membership, understand that Xenios does not promise any outcome, result, or health
improvement from the membership, its plans, its content, or any product available through it.
You accept it before you pay the $50 activation fee or authorize the $25 monthly membership.

## 2. What this acknowledgment covers

It covers everything the membership provides, including:

- the mandatory assessment and the Whole-Life Blueprint,
- Xenios 30 monthly plans and the Xenios 90 three-month roadmap,
- monthly fitness and nutrition PDFs,
- member-only Guides,
- the tracker and its metrics,
- support through Telegram,
- and any product purchased through the member catalog.

## 3. No promised outcomes

1. No outcome, result, or health improvement is promised. This includes, without limitation,
   weight change, muscle gain, body composition change, improved sleep, energy, recovery,
   cognition, mood, athletic performance, appearance, or any other measure of health or
   performance.
2. Individual results vary. Your genetics, health history, medications, sleep, stress,
   adherence, environment, and many other factors affect what happens, and most of them are
   outside Xenios's knowledge and control.
3. Plans and materials are educational. The Blueprint, Xenios 30, Xenios 90, the PDFs, and
   the Guides are educational tools that organize information and suggest a structured
   starting point. They are not medical care, not a diagnosis, and not a prescription
   (see the Research and Education Disclaimer, XR-MEM-009).
4. Tracker numbers are descriptive, not predictive. Tracker metrics describe what you log.
   They do not predict or guarantee any future result.
5. No product sold or described through Xenios Research is promised to produce any effect
   in you. No product is represented as "FDA approved", "clinically proven", or "safe for
   everyone". Peptide products are research products whose classification and permitted
   marketing lane are under formal review.
6. Any example, before-and-after comparison, testimonial, or member story you may see is one
   person's account. It is not typical-results evidence and it is not a promise to you.

## 4. What Xenios does commit to

Xenios commits to process, not results. During the founding phase this includes:

- Samuel Boadu personally reviews every initial and monthly plan, with a review commitment
  of 48 elapsed hours, including weekends,
- new fitness and nutrition PDFs each month, and a monthly Review Week after your first
  30-day plan,
- one free early plan-change request each month,
- a Telegram support response target of approximately 12 hours for normal questions.

These are service commitments. Meeting them does not create, and missing them does not imply,
any promise about your health or performance outcomes.

## 5. What this acknowledgment does not do

This acknowledgment does not waive rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, and it
does not relieve Xenios of duties imposed by law. It does not change your cancellation rights,
your privacy rights, or any consumer protection right you hold under applicable law. It is a
disclosure and your confirmation that you received and understood it.

## 6. Your acknowledgment

By accepting, you confirm each of the following:

1. I understand that no outcome, result, or health improvement is promised to me.
2. I understand that individual results vary and that my results may differ from anyone
   else's, including any example or testimonial I have seen.
3. I understand that the plans, Blueprint, PDFs, Guides, and tracker are educational and are
   not medical care, diagnosis, or prescription.
4. I understand that no product available through Xenios Research is promised to produce any
   effect in me, and that research-product classification is under formal review.
5. I understand that Xenios's commitments are commitments of process and service, not of
   results.
6. I understand this acknowledgment does not take away rights the law gives me.
