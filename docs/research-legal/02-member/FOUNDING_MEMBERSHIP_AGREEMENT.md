## 1. What this agreement is

This Founding Membership Agreement (the "Agreement") is the master agreement between you and [ENTITY], the operator of the Xenios Research program ("Xenios"). It governs your Founding Membership in Xenios Research, a private, application-based health, performance, and research membership for approved members who are 21 or older.

You are reading this because your application was approved and your identity and age were verified. You accept this Agreement before you pay anything. If you do not accept it, you do not become a member and you are not charged.

This Agreement is a draft. It is not effective until counsel approves it and Xenios formally publishes it.

## 2. Definitions

- "Member" means a person whose application was approved, whose identity and age were verified, who accepted the required agreements, who completed the activation payment and recurring authorization, and whose account is active.
- "Program" means Xenios Research, the private membership described in this Agreement.
- "Member materials" means the non-public materials made available to members, including Guides, plans, the Blueprint, fitness and nutrition PDFs, and member-only pages.
- "Founder" means Samuel Boadu, the named accountable human for the Program.
- "Founding phase" means the initial launch period of the Program during which the Founder personally reviews every initial and monthly plan.

## 4. The Program is private and application-based

Membership is not open to the public. Every member applies, is reviewed by a human, and is approved individually. The shared entrance password for /research unlocks only the entrance and application layer. It does not create membership and it does not unlock member content, prices, or purchasing.

Xenios may decline an application without stating a reason. Declining an application is not a penalty and creates no obligation on either side.

## 5. Eligibility, identity, and age

You must be 21 or older. After approval and before this Agreement is presented, Xenios verifies your identity and age. You represent that:

- the information in your application was true and complete when you gave it,
- you are the person whose identity was verified,
- you are 21 or older, and
- you will keep your account information accurate.

There is no formal appeal from a failed identity or age verification. You may retry with corrected information. The Founder does not casually override identity requirements.

## 6. Order of activation

Activation follows this order, and each step depends on the one before it:

1. Application approval.
2. Identity and age verification.
3. Acceptance of this Agreement and the incorporated documents.
4. The $50 activation payment (XR-MEM-002).
5. The $25 recurring monthly membership authorization (XR-MEM-003).
6. Password creation, then mandatory multi-factor authentication (MFA) enrollment with recovery codes.
7. Active membership.

Agreements always come before payment. No charge occurs before you have accepted the required documents.

## 7. Term and renewal

Your membership begins at activation and continues month to month. It renews automatically each month under the recurring authorization in XR-MEM-003 until you cancel or the membership is otherwise terminated under this Agreement. There is no minimum term and no long-term commitment.

## 8. Fees

- One-time activation fee: $50, charged once at activation, governed by XR-MEM-002.
- Recurring membership fee: $25 per month, governed by XR-MEM-003.

Both amounts are launch prices. They are admin-configurable, and any change applies prospectively only, with advance notice. A price change never applies to a period you have already paid for. If the monthly price changes, you will receive notice before the new price takes effect and you may cancel before it applies.

Product purchases, shipping, and any expedited or temperature-controlled shipping services are charged separately under the terms presented at checkout. The membership fee does not include products.

## 9. What membership includes

While your membership is active, the Program includes:

- access to the member website, the member catalog, and prices,
- direct purchasing of enabled, in-stock, eligible products, subject to server-side availability and lane controls,
- a mandatory assessment, due within 3 days of activation, taking about 10 minutes,
- a preliminary Whole-Life Blueprint after the assessment,
- Founder review of every initial and monthly plan during the founding phase, with a review commitment of 48 elapsed hours including weekends,
- Xenios 30 (a monthly plan) and Xenios 90 (a three-month roadmap), included for every active member,
- new fitness and nutrition PDFs each month,
- a Monthly Review Week after your first 30-day plan, with one free early plan-change request per month,
- the health tracker, which unlocks only after you complete the assessment,
- the member-only Guide library,
- support through Telegram, 24/7 intake for text and 60-second voice questions, with a normal response target of approximately 12 hours, and
- the member referral program under XR-MEM-026.

Service levels such as the 48-hour review commitment and the 12-hour support target are good-faith operational targets. They are designed to be met consistently, but they are not guarantees, and missing a target is not a breach of this Agreement.

## 10. What membership is not

Read this section carefully. It states the limits of the Program.

- The Program is not medical care. The assessment, the Blueprint, the plans, the tracker, the Guides, and support are educational and organizational tools. They are not diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, or dosing direction, and nothing in the Program directs the use of any medication.
- The Program is not an emergency service. If you have an emergency, contact emergency services (911 in the United States). Emergency language sent through support channels is routed to emergency services guidance, not to a queue.
- No outcomes are guaranteed. The Program does not promise results of any kind.
- Peptide products in the catalog are research products whose classification and permitted marketing lane are under formal review. Classification, claims, quality, and professional oversight are governed by law, not by disclaimers, and a research-use label is never paired with human-outcome promises.
- The tracker is not a medical device. Xenios does not represent itself as a HIPAA covered entity for direct-to-consumer services; the HIPAA applicability analysis is pending (see XR-POL-009).

## 12. Confidentiality

Your confidentiality obligations are stated in the Private Membership Confidentiality Covenant (XR-MEM-006). In summary, non-public member materials are shared with you for your own use and are not for redistribution. The Covenant contains carve-outs that always apply, including reports to regulators, safety reporting, legal process, professional consultation, and truthful reviews of your own experience. The Covenant controls the details.

## 13. Account security

- MFA is required for every member. You will receive recovery codes at enrollment and must store them safely. Passkeys may be offered.
- Your account is personal. Do not share credentials or let anyone else use your account.
- Telegram is a support channel only, never the system of record. Never send passwords, reset tokens, identity documents, plan PDFs, raw health media, or payment data over Telegram, and Xenios will never ask you to.
- Tell Xenios promptly at research@xeniostechnology.com if you believe your account has been compromised.

## 14. Purchases, shipping, and returns (summary)

Product purchases are separate transactions under the terms presented at checkout. As context, and subject to those terms:

- Standard shipping is $12.95 once per order, a launch working rate that is admin-configurable and reviewed against actual cost. There is no free-shipping threshold. Expedited and temperature-controlled services are priced separately when available.
- One order may ship in multiple packages from different fulfillment locations. You see one order history.
- There are no ordinary returns, for product integrity and chain-of-custody reasons. Verified damage, loss, incorrect item, missing item, or temperature compromise may receive full replacement or full refund. The final returns policy requires counsel, processor, insurer, and fulfillment review.
- Orders over $1,000, unusual quantities, or risk-rule triggers receive manual human review before fulfillment, with a target of approximately 2 hours.

## 15. Privacy and data

Xenios handles your personal information under the Xenios Research member privacy notice. Key commitments that also appear there:

- No tracker or health data is sent to advertising platforms.
- Raw government ID images are not retained unless counsel and the identity design require it.
- Progress media uses private storage, signed URLs, malware scanning, and access auditing. Face blur is image processing, not facial recognition, and no biometric templates are stored for progress media.
- Optional sexual-wellness data is separately consented and private by default.

Privacy rights survive cancellation (see XR-MEM-004).

## 16. Suspension and termination by Xenios

Xenios may suspend or terminate your membership for:

- material breach of this Agreement or an incorporated document, including the Covenants,
- untruthful application or verification information,
- failed payment that is not cured within the grace handling described in XR-MEM-003,
- conduct that creates a safety, legal, or security risk to any person or to the Program, or
- where required by law.

Where a breach is fixable and does not involve safety, security, or legal risk, Xenios will normally describe the problem and give you a chance to respond before terminating. Suspension pauses access; termination ends it. Termination is never a fine, and it does not erase the records Xenios must keep. ## 17. Cancellation by you

You can cancel at any time, self-service, from your account. Cancellation is at least as easy as signing up. The full policy is XR-MEM-004 and it controls; in summary:

- before you confirm, the flow discloses these consequences and reminds you to download your plans and data first,
- required records are retained, your privacy rights survive, and a limited non-member receipt and privacy workflow remains available.
