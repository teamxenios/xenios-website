## 1. What this document covers

These terms explain Xenios 30: the monthly exact plan included in your active membership. "Xenios" means [ENTITY], operating the Xenios Research Founding Membership. Xenios 30 sits under your Whole-Life Blueprint (XR-MEM-014) and alongside your Xenios 90 roadmap (XR-MEM-016).

## 2. What Xenios 30 is

Xenios 30 is your exact plan for the current month. It answers "what do I actually do this month" rather than "what is the general direction". Each Xenios 30 may include:

- your fitness PDF for the month,
- your nutrition PDF for the month,
- the Blueprint actions to take this month,
- your supplement Foundation,
- product guidance relevant to your goals,
- adherence targets for the month,
- the tracker metrics to log,
- and your check-in date.

Xenios 30 is produced from your assessment, your Blueprint, your tracker data, and your check-in responses. It is education and structured planning, not supervision of your execution and not medical care.

## 3. Included in membership

- Xenios 30 is included for every active member. There is no separate charge for it.
- New fitness and nutrition PDFs are published monthly while your membership is active.
- Product guidance inside a Xenios 30 never obligates you to buy anything. Purchases are always your separate decision at the prices shown in the member catalog.
- The plan may reference products excluded for you to avoid unnecessary duplication. Exclusions are planning guidance, not a restriction on your account.

## 4. The monthly refresh

1. After your first 30-day plan, your plan refreshes through the monthly Review Week (XR-MEM-019).
2. Each month's package is prepared, reviewed by Samuel during the founding phase, and published within the 48 elapsed hour review commitment described in XR-MEM-014 and XR-MEM-019. That commitment is a monitored service standard, not a contractual guarantee.
3. Each monthly document is published as a new versioned secure PDF in the Document Center, with email notification and optional Telegram notification. Prior versions are archived, not erased.
4. You will be asked to acknowledge each new document. Acknowledgment confirms receipt, not agreement with every recommendation, and it waives nothing.

## 5. A plan may stay the same on purpose

A refreshed plan is not always a changed plan. When your current plan is working and the best decision is to continue it, your next Xenios 30 may remain materially unchanged. An unchanged month is a deliberate coaching decision, not a missed update, and it is still published as a new versioned document for the month. Change is never manufactured just to look busy.

## 6. Changing the plan early

You may request one early plan change each month at no extra charge, before your regular Review Week. Early plan changes are governed by the Early Plan-Change Terms (XR-MEM-020).

## 7. Your part

Xenios 30 depends on what you disclose and log. You agree to:

- keep your allergies, restrictions, health context, and goals current,
- log the tracker metrics your plan lists, to the extent you can, and
- tell Xenios when something in the plan is not working for you, through Telegram support or a plan-change request.

Xenios relies on your disclosures and does not independently verify them. Missing tracker data does not pause your membership, but it reduces how well the next month's plan can be tuned.

## 8. Limits and safety

1. Xenios 30 is not medical care, diagnosis, treatment, or a prescription, and it contains no medication direction or dosing instructions.
2. The fitness component is governed by the Fitness Program Acknowledgment (XR-MEM-017), and the nutrition component by the Nutrition Program Acknowledgment (XR-MEM-018), including their stop-if-symptoms guidance.
3. No outcome is guaranteed. Adherence targets are targets, not promises of results.
4. Any research product referenced in product guidance remains a research product whose classification and permitted marketing lane are under formal review. Nothing in a plan changes a product's legal status or makes an outcome claim for it.
5. Emergencies go to emergency services (911 in the US). Telegram support is not an emergency channel.

These terms do not waive rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, and they do not relieve Xenios of duties imposed by law.

## 9. Cancellation

If you cancel your membership, access ends immediately, including access to current and archived Xenios 30 documents in the Document Center, and remaining paid time is forfeited, with no prorated refund unless applicable law requires one. This policy is subject to applicable law and counsel review. Download the plan PDFs you want to keep before you confirm cancellation. Cancellation does not erase records Xenios must keep, and your privacy rights survive cancellation.

## 10. Changes to these terms

Xenios may revise these terms prospectively. Material changes are presented for fresh acceptance, with the version, presented text, and timestamp recorded.
