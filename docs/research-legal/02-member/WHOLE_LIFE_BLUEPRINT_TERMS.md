## 1. What this document covers

These terms explain the Whole-Life Blueprint (the "Blueprint"): what it is, what it is not, how it is produced, how it is reviewed, what its status labels mean, and what Xenios asks of you so the Blueprint can be accurate. "Xenios" means [ENTITY], operating the Xenios Research Founding Membership. "You" means an active member.

The Blueprint is included in your active membership. There is no separate charge for it.

## 2. What the Blueprint is

The Blueprint is a personalized organizing document. It is built from your mandatory assessment and any information you later add or correct. A Blueprint may include:

- your primary goal and secondary goals,
- your top three priorities,
- a fitness program assignment,
- a nutrition program assignment,
- a supplement Foundation,
- product options relevant to your goals,
- products excluded to avoid unnecessary duplication,
- research-product pathways, described within their permitted lane,
- your Xenios 30 monthly plan and Xenios 90 roadmap (governed by XR-MEM-015 and XR-MEM-016),
- a monthly cost estimate, and
- questions where Xenios needs more information from you.

The Blueprint is designed to organize your goals and give you an exact starting point. It is education and planning support, not supervision of your day-to-day execution.

## 3. What the Blueprint is not

Read this section carefully. It states the limits of the Blueprint.

1. The Blueprint is not medical care, diagnosis, treatment, or a prescription. No part of it diagnoses a condition, prescribes anything, or directs medication or dosing.
2. The Blueprint is not an emergency service. If you have a medical emergency, contact emergency services (911 in the US).
3. The Blueprint does not guarantee any outcome. No result is promised.
4. The monthly cost estimate is an estimate for planning, not a bill, a quote, or an obligation to purchase anything. Product purchases are always your separate decision.
5. Product references in the Blueprint do not change any product's legal status. Peptide items are research products whose classification and permitted marketing lane are under formal review. Classification, claims, quality, and professional oversight are governed by law, not by disclaimers or by this document.
6. The Blueprint relies on what you disclose. Xenios does not independently verify your health information. If your information is wrong or out of date, the Blueprint can be wrong.

These terms do not waive rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, and they do not relieve Xenios of duties imposed by law.

## 4. The required assessment

The Blueprint starts with the mandatory assessment.

- The assessment is due within 3 days of activation.
- It takes approximately 10 minutes.
- It covers goals, body and routine, fitness, nutrition, sleep, energy, stress, current products, allergies and restrictions, basic safety context, budget, routine complexity, preferences, and your 30-day and 90-day direction.
- The tracker unlocks only after you complete the assessment.
- Until you complete it, your dashboard shows: membership active, catalog active, assessment due, personalized plan pending, tracker locked.
- Reminders are sent immediately, at 24 hours, at 48 hours, on day 3, and continue until the assessment is complete.

The assessment is required for personalization. It is not an automated diagnosis and it does not produce a prescription.

## 5. How your Blueprint is produced and reviewed

1. When you submit the assessment, a preliminary Blueprint is generated promptly.
2. During the founding phase, Samuel Boadu personally reviews every initial Blueprint and every monthly plan package before it is published as reviewed.
3. The review commitment is 48 elapsed hours, including weekends, measured from when the preliminary Blueprint enters review.

The 48-hour commitment is a commitment target. Xenios is built and staffed to meet it, and its performance is monitored. ## 6. Blueprint status labels

Every Blueprint you can see carries exactly one of these labels, so you always know what you are reading:

| Label | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Preliminary | Generated from your assessment. Not yet reviewed by Samuel. Useful for orientation; expect changes. |
| Samuel Review Pending | The preliminary Blueprint has entered founder review. The 48-hour review commitment is running. |
| Samuel Reviewed | Samuel has personally reviewed and approved this version. This is the version to act on. |
| Updated | A reviewed Blueprint has been revised (for example after a check-in, a plan change, or a correction). The prior version is archived, not erased. |
| More Information Needed | Xenios needs specific answers from you before the review can be completed or a section can be finalized. The needed items are listed. |

A label describes review status only. No label converts the Blueprint into medical advice or a guarantee.

## 7. Delivery, versions, and acknowledgment

- The Blueprint, the fitness plan, and the nutrition plan are published as separate secure PDFs.
- Documents are delivered through the secure Document Center, with an email notification containing a secure link and an optional Telegram notification.
- Every published document carries a version, a template version, the reviewer, the published time, a checksum, and a current or archived status.
- You will be asked to acknowledge new documents. Acknowledgment confirms you received and opened the document; it does not certify agreement with every recommendation and it does not waive any right.
- Telegram is never the system of record. Plan PDFs are not sent through Telegram; Telegram may only notify you that a document is ready.

## 8. Keeping your information current

The Blueprint is only as good as what you tell Xenios. You agree to:

- answer the assessment truthfully and completely,
- update your allergies, restrictions, health context, and goals when they change, and
- respond to "More Information Needed" items so your plan is not built on gaps.

Xenios relies on your disclosures and does not independently verify them.

## 9. Safety limits and stopping

The fitness and nutrition components of your Blueprint are governed by the Fitness Program Acknowledgment (XR-MEM-017) and the Nutrition Program Acknowledgment (XR-MEM-018), including their stop-if-symptoms guidance. In short: if something feels wrong, stop, and seek care from a qualified professional. Emergencies go to emergency services (911 in the US).

## 10. Changes to these terms

Xenios may revise these terms prospectively. Material changes are presented for fresh acceptance, with the version, presented text, and timestamp recorded. Changes never apply retroactively to an acceptance already given.

## 11. Cancellation

If you cancel your membership, access ends immediately, including access to the Blueprint and the Document Center, and remaining paid time is forfeited, with no prorated refund unless applicable law requires one. This policy is subject to applicable law and counsel review. Download the documents you want to keep before you confirm cancellation. Cancellation does not erase records Xenios must keep (transactions, payments, agreements, safety, security, and audit records are retained under the retention schedule), and your privacy rights survive cancellation.
