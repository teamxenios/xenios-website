## 1. What this document covers

These Private Entrance Terms (the "Entrance Terms") govern one thing: the private entrance to
Xenios Research at the route /research, including the shared entrance password and the
application layer behind it. They apply to every person who visits /research, whether or not
that person ever applies for membership.

Xenios Research (the "program") is a private, application-based Founding Membership operated
by Xenios ([ENTITY]). Membership is limited to approved applicants who are 21 or older.

These Entrance Terms sit alongside the Website Terms of Use (XR-PUB-002), which govern the
site as a whole. If the two conflict about the entrance or application layer, these Entrance
Terms control for that layer. [COUNSEL: confirm this precedence rule.]

## 2. Definitions

- "Entrance" means the one-screen private entrance at /research, which offers: Apply for
  Membership, Member Login, Application Status, Privacy, Terms, and Support.
- "Entrance password" means the shared password that Xenios distributes privately to
  prospective applicants to reach the entrance and application layer.
- "Application layer" means the membership application form, the application status view, and
  the informational pages linked from the entrance.

## 3. What the entrance password unlocks

The entrance password unlocks only the minimal entrance and the application layer. That means:

- the entrance screen itself,
- the membership application form,
- the application status view,
- and the public informational pages linked from the entrance (Privacy, Terms, Support).

## 4. What the entrance password does not unlock

The entrance password is not a membership credential. It does not unlock any member-facing
part of the program, including:

- the member catalog,
- product prices,
- orders,
- subscriptions,
- member-only Guides,
- the Blueprint,
- the health tracker,
- referrals,
- store credit,
- private member media,
- or any administrative function.

Member-facing access requires the full activation sequence: approval, identity and age
verification, acceptance of the required agreements, the activation payment, the recurring
membership authorization, a personal password, and mandatory multi-factor authentication.

## 5. No member rights conferred

Holding the entrance password, reaching the entrance, or submitting an application does not
make anyone a member and does not create any right to membership, products, pricing, content,
or support beyond what these Entrance Terms describe. Xenios reviews every application
individually and has no obligation to admit any applicant. Admission decisions are described
in the Application Status Terms (XR-PUB-010).

## 6. Entrance password handling

The entrance password is shared with you for your own use in reaching the entrance and
applying. You agree not to publish it, post it publicly, sell it, or distribute it in bulk.
Xenios may change or revoke the entrance password at any time, without notice, for any
reason, including suspected public exposure. A changed or revoked entrance password does not
affect the status of an application already submitted.

The entrance password is deliberately low-sensitivity: it protects nothing personal and
nothing member-facing. Do not reuse a personal password as, or confuse it with, the entrance
password. Your personal account password, created later in activation, is never shared.

## 7. Applying for membership

If you choose to apply, the application layer will present, at minimum:

- the Age 21+ Attestation (XR-PUB-008),
- the Application Accuracy Certification (XR-PUB-009),
- and the Application Status Terms (XR-PUB-010).

The application asks for identity and contact basics, goals, and background. It does not
collect government ID images, payment card data, or detailed medical records. Formal identity
and age verification happen only after approval, before any agreement or payment.

## 8. Privacy at the entrance layer

The entrance itself collects only the technical data needed to operate and secure it. The
application form collects the information it displays. How Xenios handles that information is
described in the program privacy notice linked from the entrance. Application data for
unsuccessful applicants is retained under the Retention and Deletion Schedule (XR-POL-005).

## 9. Prohibited conduct at the entrance layer

You may not:

- attempt to bypass the entrance, probe, scan, or test the entrance or application layer for
  vulnerabilities without written authorization,
- submit an application in someone else's name or on someone else's behalf,
- submit automated, bulk, or fraudulent applications,
- or scrape, harvest, or republish content from the entrance or application layer.

Xenios may block access, reject applications, and preserve records in response to any of the
above, subject to applicable law.

## 10. Changes to these terms

Xenios may update these Entrance Terms. Material changes will be posted at the entrance with
a new version number. Continued use of the entrance after a change constitutes acceptance of
the updated terms, to the extent permitted by law. [COUNSEL: confirm the change-notice

## 11. What these terms do not do

These Entrance Terms are access terms for a private entrance. They do not waive rights that
cannot be waived under applicable law, and they do not relieve Xenios of duties imposed by
law. Nothing at the entrance or in the application layer is medical advice, and nothing in
the program guarantees any outcome.

## 12. Contact

Questions about the entrance or an application: research@xeniostechnology.com. Support
boundaries, including the emergency boundary, are described in the Support and Emergency
Boundary Notice (XR-PUB-013).
