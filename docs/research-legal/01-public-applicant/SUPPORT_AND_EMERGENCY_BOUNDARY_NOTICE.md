## 1. Read this first

If you are experiencing a medical or mental-health emergency, or you believe someone is in
danger, call 911 (in the United States) or your local emergency number now. Do not use
Xenios support channels for emergencies. Xenios support is not an emergency service, is not
monitored in real time, and cannot dispatch help.

## 2. What Xenios support is

Xenios Research support answers member questions about the program: the membership, plans,
products in the catalog, orders, subscriptions, account and security, and how to use the
site. Channels:

- Members: Telegram, open for questions 24/7, by text or voice note up to 60 seconds.
- Everyone (visitors, applicants, members): email to research@xeniostechnology.com.

A named human is accountable for support. During the founding phase the founder personally
stands behind escalations; the member-facing address above is the route to reach him.

## 3. What Xenios support is not

- It is not medical care. Support does not diagnose any condition, prescribe anything,
  recommend or confirm dosing, or direct the use of any medication or compound. This applies
  to every channel and every staff member, without exception.
- It is not an emergency or crisis service. Messages can wait hours in a queue. If a message
  contains emergency language, the response directs the person to emergency services; it does
  not and cannot replace them.
- It is not a substitute for your own clinician. Questions about your health, symptoms, or
  medical decisions belong with a qualified professional who knows you.
- Guides, plans, the Blueprint, and the tracker are educational and organizational tools, not
  medical care, not a medical device, and not an emergency service.

## 4. Response times are targets, not promises

The normal response target is approximately 12 hours. That is an operational target for
ordinary questions, not a guarantee, a service-level commitment, or a promise of any specific
response time. Real response times vary with volume and review capacity, and the founding
phase is deliberately founder-operated.

Support also has no self-service urgency controls, by design:

- there is no public queue number or position indicator,
- and there is no self-mark-urgent button. Urgency is assessed by the human handling the
  queue. Anything that is truly an emergency belongs with emergency services, not the queue.

Question statuses you may see: Pending, Being Reviewed, More Information Needed, Answer
Ready, Completed. A closed question is not reopened; a follow-up creates a new linked
question. Members can rate answers.

## 5. What must never be sent over Telegram

Telegram is a convenience channel, not the system of record. Never send over Telegram:

- passwords or password-reset tokens,
- identity documents or photos of them,
- plan PDFs (these live in the secure Document Center),
- raw health media (progress photos and videos belong in the tracker, which uses private
  storage),
- or payment card or bank details.

Xenios staff will never ask for your password on any channel. If a message asks you for any
of the items above while claiming to be Xenios, treat it as fraudulent and report it to
research@xeniostechnology.com.

## 6. What this notice does not do

This notice does not waive rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, and it does not
relieve Xenios of duties imposed by law. It exists so that no one mistakes a membership
support queue for medical care or an emergency line, and so the approximately 12 hour target
is understood as a target.
