## 1. Purpose

Xenios Research admits members through individual human review. During the founding phase the
founder, Samuel Boadu, personally reviews every application. That review relies entirely on
what you tell us: your identity and contact details, your goals, your background, your reason
for joining, and your acknowledgments. This certification is your confirmation that those
answers are accurate and are your own.

A certification is a formal statement you confirm is true when you make it.

## 2. The certification

By checking the certification box and submitting the application, you certify:

1. Every answer in my application is true, accurate, and complete to the best of my
   knowledge at the time I submit it.
2. The application is about me. I wrote or approved the answers myself, and I am not
   applying on behalf of another person, and no other person is applying through me.
3. The contact details I provided (email and mobile phone) are mine and work. I understand
   that application status and membership communications go to the email address I verified.
4. Any referral source or referral code I entered is genuine.
5. I understand that Xenios will rely on these answers in deciding whether to approve my
   application, and that a material inaccuracy is grounds for denial or, if discovered after
   activation, termination of membership, subject to applicable law.

"Material" means an inaccuracy that could reasonably affect the review decision or the safe
and honest operation of the membership, for example identity, age, eligibility, or the reason
for joining. A typo in a non-essential field is not material.

## 3. Keeping information accurate

If something material changes between submission and a decision (for example your contact
details or eligibility), tell Xenios at research@xeniostechnology.com. After activation,
members are expected to keep account information accurate under the member agreements.

## 4. Consequences of material inaccuracy

- Before a decision: the application may be denied. Denial for inaccuracy does not prevent a
  future retry with corrected information, as described in the Application Status Terms
  (XR-PUB-010).
- After activation: a material inaccuracy discovered later is grounds for termination of
  membership, handled under the membership termination terms and subject to applicable law.
  Where the issue is fixable, the normal course is to explain the problem and give you a
  chance to respond before terminating.
- Records connected to the inaccuracy are preserved under the retention schedule.

## 5. What this certification does not do

This certification does not waive rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, and it
does not relieve Xenios of duties imposed by law. It does not make you a member, does not
guarantee approval, and creates no obligation for Xenios to admit you. It also does not turn
your application answers into promises about outcomes: the program guarantees no results to
anyone.

## 6. Records

The certification is recorded server-side with a timestamp, the document version you saw, and
your applicant reference, alongside your application answers. These records survive denial,
withdrawal, and later cancellation to the extent the retention schedule requires.
