## 1. Scope

This disclosure covers the shipping services priced separately from the standard $12.95
per-order rate: Expedited 2-Day, Next-Day, Same-Day, and Temperature-Controlled. It supplements
the Shipping Policy (XR-COM-004). These services exist because some products, destinations, and
member needs justify faster or specially handled transit, and because those services cannot
responsibly be hidden inside one low flat rate.

## 2. Pricing: live or configured carrier rates

- Expedited 2-Day and Next-Day are priced at the live carrier rate or a configured carrier rate
  for the specific shipment. The price you see at checkout is the price for that shipment, based
  on the shipment's origin, destination, weight, dimensions, and service.
- Same-Day is priced from an actual courier quote for the specific delivery.
- Temperature-Controlled is priced at a separate live or configured rate that reflects the
  validated packaging and service it requires.
- Xenios does not subsidize high-cost express services. These prices are pass-through or
  configured rates, not flat fees, and they vary by order.

## 3. Expedited 2-Day and Next-Day

- Offered only when the products in the order permit that service under their shipping profiles
  and a carrier commitment is available for the destination.
- The service name describes carrier transit time after the shipment leaves the fulfillment
  owner, not time from order placement. Order cutoff times, manual review of large orders, and
  fulfillment acceptance all come before transit starts.
- Delivery timing rules in the Shipping Policy apply: no specific delivery date is displayed
  until inventory is reserved, the fulfillment owner accepts, the service is selected, the
  cutoff is known, and a carrier commitment is available. Weekend limitations are shown where
  they apply.

## 4. Same-Day

Same-Day is not a universal feature. It is offered only when all of the following are true for
the specific order:

1. the destination ZIP code is eligible,
2. the required inventory is at a location that can serve that destination,
3. a local or national courier is available,
4. the order is placed before the applicable cutoff,
5. a secure handoff at delivery is possible, and
6. an actual courier quote is available and accepted at checkout.

If any condition fails, Same-Day simply does not appear as an option. Same-Day availability on
one order does not imply availability on the next.

## 5. Temperature-Controlled

- Temperature-Controlled service is offered only for packout configurations that have passed
  validation. "Packout validation" means documented testing of the packaging configuration
  (coolant, insulation, payload, package size) against the intended lane distance, transit time,
  season, and worst-case delay, with retained protocols, results, and temperature curves.
- Xenios makes no temperature-performance claims without validation data. If a product or lane
  has no validated packout, Temperature-Controlled service is not offered for it, even on
  request.
- Some products require temperature control under their shipping profile. For those products,
  checkout applies the requirement automatically; members cannot deselect it.
- Shipments may include a temperature monitor or indicator where the product's profile requires
  one. If a delivered shipment shows a possible temperature problem (for example, a tripped
  indicator, melted coolant on a shipment that required it, or a package that arrives warm when
  it should not be), do not assume the product is fine and do not assume it is ruined. Report it
  under the Damage, Loss, Incorrect Item, and Temperature Concern Policy (XR-COM-008) so it can
  be evaluated against approved data. Support does not guess about temperature exposure.

## 6. Weekends, holidays, and carrier performance

Some services do not deliver on weekends or holidays, and some destinations have limited
service days. Checkout shows weekend limitations where they apply. Carrier and courier
performance is outside Xenios's control. A paid expedited service that the carrier fails to
meet is handled with the member and the carrier case by case, and any credit or adjustment
remains subject to carrier terms and applicable law. [COUNSEL: confirm what Xenios may and
