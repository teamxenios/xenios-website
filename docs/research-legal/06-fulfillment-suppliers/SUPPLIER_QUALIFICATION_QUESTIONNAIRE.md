## 1. Purpose and scope

This questionnaire qualifies any supplier or manufacturer whose product Xenios Research offers to members. It applies to the peptide and research product lane (currently 15 SKU records, P001 to P015, held and fulfilled by the initial fulfillment partner under XR-FUL-001), to supplement suppliers (up to 50 candidate SKUs from brands such as Momentous and Pure Encapsulations, none sold until written reseller authorization and related confirmations exist), and to any future product source. Peptide products are research products whose classification and permitted marketing lane are under formal review; a completed questionnaire supports, but does not replace, the written classification memorandum Xenios prepares for every SKU. Supplier answers about classification do not control that memorandum.

No SKU from a supplier goes on sale until this questionnaire is completed, reviewed, and rated Approved under Section 12, and the SKU's own lane approvals are in place. Quantum remains Coming Soon with commerce disabled; this questionnaire may be used to collect Quantum supplier information but does not authorize Quantum commerce.

## 2. How to complete this questionnaire

1. Answer every question in writing. Attach documents where a question says "attach".
2. Answers must be supportable by records. Verbal-only claims, especially about shelf life, are not accepted.
3. If a question does not apply to your product type, write "not applicable" and say why.
4. Sign the attestation in Section 11. Return everything through the agreed secure channel.

## 12. Scoring and disqualifying answers (Xenios internal use)

1. Disqualifying answers, each blocking approval until resolved in writing: refusal to identify the manufacturer (A4), missing written reseller authorization for a supplement SKU (B3, blocks that SKU only), no lot traceability (C4), no per-lot COA (D1), verbal-only shelf-life claims (E1), no documented excursion assessment for cold-chain SKUs (F3), inability to trace a lot to shipments (G1), refusal of concern notification or written serious adverse event allocation (G4), no product liability insurance (H1).
2. Approved: no disqualifying answers, evidence attached, and the reviewer records name and date on the supplier file. Conditional approval (open items with committed dates, no disqualifiers) may be granted only by the founder. Anything else is Rejected.
3. Approval of the supplier is necessary but not sufficient: each SKU still requires its classification memorandum and lane approvals before sale.
4. Re-qualify at renewal, after any recall or quality failure, and on material change (new manufacturer, new facility, changed formulation).
