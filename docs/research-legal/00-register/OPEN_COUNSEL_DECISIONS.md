## 1. Purpose and method

This register consolidates every open counsel item raised across the Xenios Research
document set into one numbered decision list. The 25 drafting batches produced 346 raw
open-counsel items. This document deduplicates and clusters them into 64 consolidated
decisions, numbered OCD-001 through OCD-064 and grouped by theme. Each raw item maps to
exactly one consolidated decision, and each consolidated decision lists the union of
every document key its merged raw items named.

How to read an entry:

- **Decision** states the consolidated question counsel must answer.
- **Priority** classifies urgency (definitions in Section 2), with the blocking scope named.
- **Affected documents** lists every document key from the merged raw items. Ranges are
  inclusive and verified: "XR-MEM-001 to XR-MEM-020" means every key in that span.
- **Draft position awaiting confirmation** states the position the current drafts embody,
  where one exists. It is recorded for counsel's convenience only. It is the draft
  position awaiting confirmation, not advice, and nothing becomes effective without
  counsel approval and formal publication.

Resolution workflow: when counsel resolves a decision, the resolution, date, and
resolving authority are recorded against the OCD number, the affected documents are
updated, and this register is re-versioned. New decisions counsel opens are appended
with the next OCD number.
