## 1. What this notice covers

This notice explains the current status of Quantum inside the Xenios Research Founding
Membership. It applies to every member who views the Quantum product page. It is an
informational notice. Viewing it does not create any agreement, order, or obligation.

## 2. Quantum is Coming Soon

Quantum is displayed to members in a "Coming Soon" state. That state means exactly this:

- You can view the Quantum product page.
- You can join the Quantum interest list (see the Quantum Interest List Terms, XR-QTM-002).
- You cannot order Quantum. There is no checkout, no cart, no purchase button, and no way
  to pay for Quantum anywhere on the member website.
- No price is set. Any number you may see elsewhere is not a Xenios price and is not a
  commitment.
- No availability date is set. Xenios does not promise that Quantum will become available
  at any particular time, or at all.

## 3. Why commerce is disabled

Quantum requires a formal review before Xenios can offer it. That review covers, at
minimum: the exact product identity, its manufacturer, its regulatory classification,
the claims that may lawfully be made about it, who may administer it and in what kind of
facility, whether a licensed professional must be involved, in which states it may be
offered, the quality documentation behind it, and the structure of any future transaction.

Until every element of that review is complete and formally approved, Quantum commerce
stays disabled at the server level, not just hidden in the interface. This is a
compliance control, not a marketing choice. A "research use" label or a disclaimer does
not change this: classification, claims, quality, and professional oversight are governed
by law, not by disclaimers.

## 4. What the Coming Soon page is not

- It is not an offer to sell, and nothing on it is an invitation to order.
- It is not a claim that Quantum is FDA approved, clinically proven, or safe for everyone.
  Quantum's classification and permitted marketing lane are under formal review.
- It is not medical care. Nothing on the page is a diagnosis, a prescription, dosing
  guidance, or medication direction. If you have a medical question, speak with a
  licensed clinician. Emergencies go to emergency services (911 in the US).
- It is not a waitlist position or a reservation. Joining the interest list records your
  interest and nothing more (see XR-QTM-002).

## 5. What happens if Quantum is approved

If the formal review concludes and Quantum commerce is approved for activation, Xenios
will, before any sale:

- publish approved product information and any required agreements or consents,
- notify interest list members that the status has changed,
- and enable commerce only through the approved lane, which may involve conditions such
  as professional involvement or state restrictions, depending on what counsel approves.

Interest list membership does not guarantee access, priority, pricing, or any allocation.

## 6. Changes and questions

Xenios may update this notice as the review progresses. The current version is always the
one published on the member website. Questions go to research@xeniostechnology.com.
