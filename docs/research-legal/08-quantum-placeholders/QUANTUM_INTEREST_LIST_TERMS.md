## 1. What the interest list is

The Quantum interest list is a simple record that you, an active member, want to be told
when Quantum's status changes. Quantum is currently Coming Soon and cannot be ordered
(see the Quantum Coming Soon Terms, XR-QTM-001). Joining the list is optional and free.

## 2. What we collect when you join

Joining the list records only:

- your member reference (which links to your existing member account),
- the fact that you joined the interest list,
- the timestamp of joining,
- the version of these terms you accepted,
- and your notification preference for Quantum status updates.

We do not collect any new health information, payment information, or identity documents
when you join. The interest list is not a health questionnaire and not an order form.

## 3. No obligation in either direction

- Joining does not obligate you to buy anything, now or later.
- Joining does not obligate Xenios to sell you anything, to make Quantum available, or to
  make it available to you specifically. Quantum's availability depends on a formal
  review that has not concluded, and the outcome of that review may include conditions
  (for example, professional involvement or state restrictions) or may be that Quantum is
  not offered at all.

## 4. No positions, no promises

- The interest list has no queue numbers, no ranks, and no reserved positions.
- Joining early does not create priority, guaranteed allocation, or special pricing.
- Xenios does not promise a launch date, a price, or a restock-style availability date.
- If Quantum is later offered under conditions set by counsel, eligibility will be
  determined by those approved conditions, not by interest list order.

## 5. What you will receive

If you are on the list, Xenios will notify you when Quantum's member-facing status
changes (for example, from Coming Soon to an approved offering state, or to a
discontinued state). Notification uses your existing member contact channels and your
notification preferences. Status notifications are program communications tied to your
membership; they are not marketing for other products.

## 6. Leaving the list

You can leave the interest list at any time:

- from the Quantum product page or your account settings, with immediate effect, or
- by emailing research@xeniostechnology.com from your member email.

Leaving stops future Quantum status notifications. It does not affect your membership,
your other subscriptions, or anything else in your account. A record that you joined and
left may be retained under the retention schedule for audit purposes.

## 7. Privacy

Interest list data is member account data. It is handled under the Xenios Research
privacy notice, is not sold, and is not sent to advertising platforms. It is used only to
manage the list and send the notifications described above.

## 8. General

These terms do not waive rights that cannot be waived under applicable law, and they do
not relieve Xenios of duties imposed by law. Xenios may update these terms prospectively
with notice; the version you accepted is recorded with your acceptance.
