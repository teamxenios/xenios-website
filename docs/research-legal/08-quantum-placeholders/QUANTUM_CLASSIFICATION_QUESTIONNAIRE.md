## 1. Purpose

This questionnaire captures the facts counsel needs to classify Quantum before any
commerce decision. It is an internal working document. Every conclusion field is reserved
for counsel. Xenios (operations) fills in the factual answer fields where the facts are
known; counsel fills the analysis and conclusion fields marked [COUNSEL]. No field in this
document authorizes any sale, claim, or activation. Quantum stays Coming Soon until the
Quantum Commerce Activation Checklist (XR-QTM-008) is complete.

## 2. How to use this document

- Operations answers the "Answer" fields with facts only, no opinions on legal status.
- If a fact is unknown, write "Unknown" and route it as a gap, do not guess.
- Every "Conclusion" field is [COUNSEL] and must be completed by qualified counsel.
- Do not describe Quantum as FDA approved, clinically proven, or safe for everyone in any
  answer. Classification is the question, not the assumption.
- Do not record any diagnosis, prescribing, or dosing content here.
