# Research UI Direction

**Status:** active direction for UI-002
**Updated:** 2026-07-18

## Desired feeling

- Editorial, institutional, scientific, selective, human, spacious, modern, restrained, and unmistakably part of xenios.
- Premium through proportion, typography, restraint, and clarity rather than visual noise.

## Source of truth

- The current main Xenios site and its actual shared components, tokens, spacing, navigation, footer, and motion patterns.
- The canonical V2 membership specification for product architecture and safety constraints.

## Layout preferences

- Clear narrative sections with intentional variation.
- Strong whitespace and controlled line length.
- Accessible whole-life diagrams and evidence maps.
- Avoid endless identical card grids, dashboard styling on editorial pages, fixed-height content cards, and clipped horizontal navigation.

## Typography direction

- Reuse the main site's font families and type tokens.
- Large editorial headings, legible body copy, fluid sizing, balanced wrapping, and safe handling of long scientific names.

## Image direction

- Original xenios-owned editorial diagrams, whole-life visuals, and verified product renders.
- No supplier branding, copied competitor imagery, syringes, injection imagery, bodily transformation imagery, or unlicensed assets.
- Build the referral passport and social-share treatment as data-driven HTML, CSS, and SVG so identity, code, QR payload, and status remain controlled by the product.

## Animation direction

- Motion should clarify hierarchy or state.
- Respect reduced motion and avoid page-blocking or decorative continuous animation.

## Mobile expectations

- Intentional layouts at 320 px and above.
- No page-level horizontal overflow, clipped controls, crowded navigation, or sticky elements covering content.

## Priority pages

1. Responsive foundation and Research shell
2. Membership and comparison
3. Referral explanation, invitation card, invite landing, and member referral UI states
4. Trust and data-use explanation
5. Blueprint, programs, ambassadors, and professionals

## Referral visual direction

- Black, warm white, graphite, silver, and one controlled Xenios accent.
- Editorial hierarchy with small mono labels, meaningful reference numbers, and 4 px geometry.
- Subtle scientific grid, registration marks, or grain created in code.
- Clean, functional QR presentation with no sensitive payload.
- A more restrained, system-oriented composition than the supplied Superpower example.
- Mobile-first share controls with large targets and safe wrapping.
- Applicant and active-member variants must feel related but materially different.
