# Accepted decision: Keep Research private during infrastructure completion

**Decision owner:** Samuel
**Recorded by:** CODEX_UI
**Date:** 2026-07-18
**Status:** accepted for the current build cycle

## Decision

Keep the entire Xenios Research surface access-controlled and noindexed while the membership dashboard, secure applicant access, referral accounting, privacy controls, and Blueprint workflow are completed.

CODEX_UI may build the requested public-facing business presentation inside that private review environment. This does not approve peptide, GLP-1, Quantum, product, or clinical content for public launch.

## Consequences

- Existing password-gate and noindex contracts remain intact.
- New membership, referral, trust, Blueprint, program, and professional routes are preview surfaces, not a public launch.
- Regulated product routes remain subject to brand, legal, compliance, evidence, and access review.
- A later decision must define which general whole-life pages can become public and which content stays private.
- PR language and screenshots must not imply that payment, referral credits, member authentication, or privacy workflows are already operational.
