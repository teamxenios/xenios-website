# TB-500 Regulatory Status

**Status checked: 2026-07-19. This page must be re-verified before publication, and
re-verified again at each review cycle. Regulatory status changes without notice, and a
substantial part of this page could not be verified at all on the date checked.**

Every statement below carries a jurisdiction, a date checked, and a source URL. Source ids
refer to SOURCE_REGISTRY.md.

**Approval, registration, listing or prohibition in one jurisdiction is not approval,
registration, listing or prohibition in another.** Each jurisdiction decides independently.
Nothing on this page should be read as describing the position of a jurisdiction not named
here, and most jurisdictions are not named here because they were not checked.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## Reading this page

The compound at issue is TB-500, the synthetic seven-amino-acid acetylated fragment
Ac-LKKTETQ. Where a statement concerns full-length thymosin beta-4 or a formulated product
built from it, that is stated in the row. The two are not interchangeable, and their
regulatory records are not interchangeable either.

## What may be said to a member as of 2026-07-19

Permitted, in this wording or close to it:

- TB-500 is explicitly named on the WADA Prohibited List and is prohibited at all times for
  athletes under anti-doping jurisdiction, in and out of competition.
- Validated detection methods for TB-500 and its metabolites have been published, so the
  anti-doping risk is documented rather than theoretical.
- No approved TB-500 product was located in this search, and the only registered human study
  is ongoing with no results. FDA sources could not be retrieved on the date checked, so this
  must be confirmed by a human before publication.
- Regulatory status differs by country, and this Guide checked only US anti-doping and, at a
  distance, the international anti-doping position.

Not permitted, in any wording:

- Any FDA compounding status claim, in either direction.
- Any description of the GenoGenix warning letter.
- Any specific WADA section number, until the current list is read.
- Any statement about a jurisdiction not named on this page.
- Any implication that a regulatory or supply category says something about efficacy or
  safety. The framing "research use only" must not be used to imply human benefit.
