- [ ] **Every** preclinical finding names its species or model in the same sentence as the
      finding. Reviewer has read every sentence containing a research finding and confirmed this
      individually.
- [ ] No bare mechanism sentence appears anywhere that could read as human fact.
- [ ] Rat findings are labelled as rat findings.
- [ ] Cell-culture and fibroblast findings are labelled as cultured cell findings.
- [ ] Equine findings are labelled as equine findings and are not presented as evidence of

## 5. The fragment versus full-length distinction

This is the central accuracy issue for this compound. It gets its own section.

- [ ] The distinction between TB-500 (7-amino-acid fragment), thymosin beta-4 (43-amino-acid
      full-length protein), and formulated investigational products (RGN-259, NL005) is made
      early and explicitly.
- [ ] Every human trial cited in the packet is labelled with which molecule it studied.
- [ ] No thymosin beta-4 trial is presented anywhere as TB-500 evidence.
- [ ] The route of every cited human trial (eye surface, wound surface) is stated alongside its
      result.
- [ ] The venous ulcer paper is correctly described as reporting no outcome data, and is not
      cited as showing accelerated healing.
- [ ] The neurotrophic keratopathy result correctly states that the day 29 primary comparison did
      not reach statistical significance.
- [ ] Sample sizes are stated for every cited human trial.
