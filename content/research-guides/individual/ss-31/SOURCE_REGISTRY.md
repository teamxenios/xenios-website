# SS-31 Source Registry

Every source consulted for the SS-31 (elamipretide) Guide, with its retrieval status. Sources
marked UNVERIFIED were not successfully read in full and cannot carry a claim on their own.
Sources marked NON-EVIDENCE exist only to record where an unusable claim originated.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## Verification key

- **VERIFIED 2026-07-21**: retrieved and read in this research session at the URL shown.
- **PARTIAL 2026-07-21**: retrieved at one level (for example a registry record or a
  bibliographic listing) but not at the level a specific claim would require.
- **UNVERIFIED**: fetch failed, was blocked, or returned unparseable or empty content. Existence
  may be known from metadata, but the content was not read.
- **SPONSOR-INTERESTED**: retrieved and read, but authored by a party with a commercial interest
  in the conclusion. Admissible for the fact and date of an event, never for an assessment of
  the evidence.
- **NON-EVIDENCE**: disqualified as a source of fact by the exclusion rules in SOURCE_PLAN.md.
