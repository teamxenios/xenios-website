**No single person may sign more than one role.** This rule is not administrative tidiness. A
five-gate review exists so that five independent judgements have to agree, and one person holding
two pens defeats it entirely: the same blind spot, the same incentive, and the same tiredness pass
through both gates unchallenged. A reviewer who is asked to cover a second role must decline and
escalate rather than sign twice. If a role cannot be staffed, the Guide does not publish. An
unstaffed role is a blocked gate, never a waived one.

## Standing rules for this gate

1. **No single person may sign more than one reviewer role.** See the note above the sign-off table.
   One self-approving actor defeats a five-gate review entirely.
2. **AI drafts never publish automatically.** This document set was drafted by an AI system from a
   research record. It carries no editorial authority and is not member-facing until human sign-off
   is complete.
3. **An unchecked box blocks publication.** There is no override and no expedited path.
4. **A fabricated or unverifiable citation is the most serious possible failure**, because a
   reviewer or a member may trust it. If a citation cannot be confirmed at source, it is removed and
   logged as a gap. Omitting is always better than guessing.
5. **Concluding that the evidence is old, small, and negative is a successful outcome**, not a
   failure of the research. The Guide must not be softened to sound more balanced than the record.
6. **A verified=false flag may never be quietly dropped.** It is closed by retrieval or it stays
   visible. Removing a flag without doing the work is a falsification of the record.
7. **Regulatory status expires.** Any Guide published with a regulatory date more than 90 days old
   returns to this gate for re-verification. The July 2026 FDA advisory committee consideration
   makes this compound specifically time-sensitive.
8. **Material changes restart the relevant section.** Any edit to an evidence, safety, or regulatory
   statement after sign-off requires that section to be re-checked and re-signed.
