## Standing rules for this gate

1. **AI drafts never publish automatically.** This document set was drafted by an AI system from a
   research record. It carries no editorial authority and is not member-facing until human sign-off is
   complete.
2. **An unchecked box blocks publication.** There is no override and no expedited path.
3. **A fabricated or unverifiable citation is the most serious possible failure**, because a reviewer
   or a member may trust it. If a citation cannot be confirmed at source, it is removed and logged as
   a gap. Omitting is always better than guessing.
4. **Concluding that human evidence is absent is a successful outcome**, not a failure of the research.
   An honest empty evidence table is the correct result for this compound and must not be padded.
5. **Regulatory status expires.** Any Guide published with a regulatory date more than 90 days old
   returns to this gate for re-verification. The pending FDA advisory committee review makes this
   compound specifically time-sensitive.
6. **Material changes restart the relevant section.** Any edit to an evidence, safety, or regulatory
   statement after sign-off requires that section to be re-checked and re-signed.
