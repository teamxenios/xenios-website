# KPV: quality and documentation requirements

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## Purpose of this document

This document does two separate things, and the distinction matters.

First, it states what quality documentation **should** exist before any product containing this
compound could be taken seriously by Xenios. That standard is written independently of KPV, and it
applies to any supplier presenting any material.

Second, it records the **honest current state** for KPV specifically. Xenios has not confirmed any of
these items for this compound. Every checkbox below is unchecked, and none is expected to be checked
in the near term.

This is not a supplier evaluation, because there is no evaluation under way. It is the standard a
supplier would have to meet before one could begin.

## The starting position

Two facts frame everything below.

1. **KPV is not an approved drug in any jurisdiction that was checked** (checked 2026-07-19). There is
   therefore no approved manufacturer, no approved specification, and no regulatory authority
   inspecting a production line against a filed standard.

2. **No KPV-specific purity, identity, or counterfeit analysis could be located.** No peer-reviewed
   study testing material sold as KPV was found during research. Nobody appears to have systematically
   checked what is in these vials. That is an absence of information, not a favourable result.

What is documented is how the unregulated channel performs generally. A peer-reviewed 2024
market-surveillance study in the Journal of Medical Internet Research identified 317 online pharmacy
links, of which 134 were illegal. Test purchases from six illegal pharmacies yielded three injection
vials, all considered probable substandard or falsified products. Visual inspection showed
non-compliance on 59 to 63 percent of criteria. Measured active content exceeded the label by 28.56 to
38.69 percent, while measured purity was 7.7 to 14.37 percent against a claimed 99 percent, and
endotoxin was detected in every sample (PMID 39509151, verified 2026-07-19). **That study tested
semaglutide, not KPV.** It is evidence about the supply channel and is cited only as such.

A note on numbers that will be encountered elsewhere: research for this Guide surfaced several
striking gray-market failure statistics covering sequence errors, heavy metals, residual solvents, and
mass audit failure rates. Every one traced to vendor blogs, Substack posts, or supplement marketing,
with no locatable primary source. They are excluded here and must not be added.

## The checklist

Every item below is a requirement a supplier would have to satisfy, with documentation, before any
further conversation. Status for KPV as of 2026-07-19 is **NOT CONFIRMED** for every single item.

### A. Identity

- [ ] **A1. Unambiguous chemical identity of the exact substance.** Not the name "KPV". The specific
      form, since regulatory filings treat KPV free base and KPV acetate as separate bulk substances.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **A2. Verified registry identifiers.** A CAS number and molecular weight confirmed against a
      primary chemical registry. Figures circulating on vendor pages were not confirmed during
      research and are not reproduced. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
      [UNVERIFIED - background knowledge, requires human source check]
- [ ] **A3. Confirmed amino acid sequence for the specific batch**, by an orthogonal analytical method
      (for example mass spectrometry combined with sequencing), not by supplier assertion.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **A4. Explicit exclusion of confusable compounds.** Documented evidence that the material is KPV
      and not KdPT, not (CKPV)2, not full-length alpha-MSH, and not a melanocortin receptor agonist.
      This confusion is common in commercial material and is a real identity risk.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **A5. Salt form, counter-ion, and water content stated and measured.** Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **A6. A written specification the batch is tested against**, with acceptance criteria set before
      testing rather than after. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.

### B. Purity and impurities

- [ ] **B1. Quantified purity by a validated, stated method**, with the method, column, and conditions
      disclosed, not a bare percentage. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **B2. A named impurity profile**, listing what the other material is, not only how much of it
      there is. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **B3. Peptide-related impurities characterised**, including truncated, deletion, and modified
      sequences arising from synthesis. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **B4. Residual solvents quantified** against a recognised limit standard.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **B5. Elemental impurities and heavy metals quantified** against a recognised limit standard.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **B6. Aggregation assessed.** Peptide clumping is a recognised contributor to immune reactions
      against a peptide. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.

### C. Sterility and microbiological safety

- [ ] **C1. Sterility testing by a recognised method**, where the product is intended for any route
      requiring it. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **C2. Bacterial endotoxin testing with a stated limit and a measured result.** The channel
      evidence cited above found endotoxin in every sample of a different peptide tested, which makes
      this a priority item rather than a formality. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **C3. Bioburden control documented at the appropriate manufacturing stages.**
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **C4. Container closure integrity demonstrated.** Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **C5. Aseptic process validation, or a validated terminal sterilisation process, documented.**
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.

### D. Stability

- [ ] **D1. Stability data under stated storage conditions**, over a stated duration, using a
      stability-indicating method. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **D2. A justified expiry or retest date derived from that data**, rather than assigned by
      convention. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **D3. In-use stability after any preparation step.** Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **D4. Photostability and temperature-excursion data**, including behaviour during shipping.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **D5. Degradation products identified and quantified**, not just a decline in assay value.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.

### E. Chain of custody and manufacturing provenance

- [ ] **E1. Named manufacturer and named physical manufacturing site.** Not a brand, not a
      distributor, not a marketing entity. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **E2. Regulatory inspection history of that site**, and the quality standard it operates to.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **E3. Documented chain of custody from synthesis through to the finished unit**, including every
      intermediate handler. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **E4. Batch and lot traceability**, so that any single unit can be traced back to its
      manufacturing record. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **E5. Documented storage and transport conditions across the whole chain**, including
      temperature records. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **E6. Raw material provenance for starting materials and reagents.**
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.

### F. Certificate of analysis and third-party verification

- [ ] **F1. A batch-specific certificate of analysis (COA)** matching the exact lot in hand, not a
      representative or example COA for a different batch. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **F2. The COA issued by a named, accredited laboratory**, with its accreditation stated and
      independently checkable. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **F3. Test methods named on the COA**, with acceptance criteria and measured results, not
      "pass". Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **F4. Independent third-party confirmatory testing**, commissioned by a party with no commercial
      interest in the result, on a sample drawn from the same lot.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **F5. COA authenticity verifiable directly with the issuing laboratory.** COAs are forged.
      A document that cannot be confirmed at source is not evidence. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **F6. Retention samples kept and available for re-testing.** Status: NOT CONFIRMED.

### G. Labelling and claims

- [ ] **G1. Label content matching the tested content**, within a stated tolerance.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **G2. No efficacy or safety claims on the label or accompanying material** that exceed the
      evidence, which for KPV means no human efficacy or safety claims at all.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **G3. No use of "research use only" framing as an implicit signal of human benefit.** That
      framing is prohibited on any Xenios surface. Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **G4. Accurate regulatory status stated, with jurisdiction and date.**
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.

### H. Governance and adverse events

- [ ] **H1. A documented adverse event reporting route**, with a named responsible party.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **H2. A recall and market withdrawal procedure**, tested rather than merely written.
      Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **H3. Documented complaint handling and deviation records.** Status: NOT CONFIRMED.
- [ ] **H4. Insurance and liability position documented.** Status: NOT CONFIRMED.

**Xenios has not confirmed any quality documentation item for KPV.** No supplier has been assessed
against this checklist, no certificate of analysis has been reviewed, no independent testing has been
commissioned, and no material has been examined.

An unchecked box means unverified. It does not mean failed, and it certainly does not mean passed.
The purpose of publishing the checklist while every item is unmet is that a member can see exactly
what has and has not been established, rather than inferring quality from the fact that a Guide
exists.

Quality documentation is also a separate question from whether the compound does anything. Perfect
documentation for KPV would establish that a vial contains what it claims. It would not create the
human evidence that, as set out in the Guide, does not exist.
