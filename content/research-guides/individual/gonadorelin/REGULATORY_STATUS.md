# Gonadorelin Regulatory Status

**Status checked on 2026-07-19 (workflow review date). The underlying retrievals were performed on 2026-07-20, which is the date recorded against each statement below. This status must be re-verified before publication and re-verified again at any republication. Regulatory listings, marketing statuses, and prohibited lists change without notice, and several of the documents cited here are periodically updated files at a stable URL, which means the URL can remain valid while its contents change.**

Approval, listing, or registration in one jurisdiction is not approval, listing, or registration in any other. Nothing in this document describes permissibility for personal use, and nothing here is legal advice.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

| Date checked | 2026-07-20 |

| Verification | VERIFIED, full text extracted and searched |

**Unresolved and flagged for regulatory counsel.** The absence of gonadorelin from the 503A nominations list is a verified fact about that document. It is not, by itself, a conclusion about whether pharmacy compounding of gonadorelin is permitted or prohibited. Secondary sources circulating online assert a 503A Category 1 listing that the primary document does not contain, and a separate reading, that section 503A may permit compounding from bulk substances that are components of approved products without nomination, could not be verified from any retrieved source. The Guide must not assert 503A status in either direction until a human with regulatory expertise resolves this. See CONTRADICTIONS.md section 1.
