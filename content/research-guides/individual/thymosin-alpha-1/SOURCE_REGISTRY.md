## 3. Disqualified source types encountered

The following were encountered during the session and excluded from evidence use entirely.
They are listed so a reviewer knows what was rejected and does not re-import it.

- Peptide vendor and reseller pages asserting a completed February 2026 reclassification
  of thymosin alpha-1 out of FDA Category 2.
- Peptide vendor and directory pages asserting anti-doping status.
- Vendor and clinic pages presenting orphan drug designation as though it were approval.
- Vendor and clinic pages presenting approval outside the United States as though it were
  United States approval.
- Testing service marketing pages quoting gray market purity failure rates.
- Search engine snippet metadata for documents that could not be opened. Recorded in
  SOURCE_PLAN.md as leads for a human reviewer only.

## 4. Standing note on citation integrity

No PMID, NCT number, DOI, author name, journal, year, or URL in this Guide was inferred,
reconstructed, or guessed. Where a fact could not be tied to a retrieved source, the claim
was dropped and logged as a gap rather than softened into publishable language.
