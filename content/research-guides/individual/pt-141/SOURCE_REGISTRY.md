## Source grading key used in this registry

- Primary regulatory: the approved label as published in the structured product label system.
- Peer-reviewed: indexed journal publication.
- Registry: trial registration records.
- Bibliographic: a citation retrieved without its contents.
- Tertiary: encyclopedia or summary content, background only, never evidence.
- Disqualified and therefore absent: vendor, retailer, affiliate, clinic marketing, testing
  laboratory marketing, forum, and social media content.
