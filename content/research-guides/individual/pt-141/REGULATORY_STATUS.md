# Regulatory Status: PT-141 (bremelanotide)

**Status checked on: 2026-07-19.**
**This status must be re-verified before publication and before any member-facing surface
uses it. Regulatory positions change, and several items below could not be verified at all
in this session.**

Approval or registration in one jurisdiction is not approval or registration in another.
An approval also attaches to a specific product, for a specific indication, in a specific
population. It does not attach to a molecule in the abstract, and it does not travel to
other products containing the same molecule.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

---

## United States

### US-1. Approval status

**Jurisdiction:** United States, Food and Drug Administration.
**Statement:** Bremelanotide is the active ingredient in an FDA-approved prescription
injectable drug product marketed as VYLEESI. The approved label states an initial US
approval year of 2019.
**Date checked:** 2026-07-19.
**Source:** https://dailymed.nlm.nih.gov/dailymed/fda/fdaDrugXsl.cfm?setid=8c9607a2-5b57-4a59-b159-cf196deebdd9
**Verification:** VERIFIED 2026-07-19 from the FDA-authored label content published in the
structured product label system.

### US-2. Approved indication

**Jurisdiction:** United States, FDA.
**Statement:** The approved indication is the treatment of premenopausal women with
acquired, generalized hypoactive sexual desire disorder, characterised by low sexual desire
that causes marked distress or interpersonal difficulty, and that is not due to a
co-existing medical or psychiatric condition, to problems within the relationship, or to the
effects of a medicine or drug substance. The label defines acquired as the condition
developing in a person who previously had no problems with sexual desire, and generalized as
the condition occurring regardless of the type of stimulation, the situation, or the partner.
**Date checked:** 2026-07-19.
**Source:** https://dailymed.nlm.nih.gov/dailymed/fda/fdaDrugXsl.cfm?setid=8c9607a2-5b57-4a59-b159-cf196deebdd9
**Verification:** VERIFIED 2026-07-19.
**Note:** Every qualifier in that indication is load-bearing. Dropping any one of them
widens the approval to cover people it does not cover.

### US-3. Limitations of use stated on the approved label

**Jurisdiction:** United States, FDA.
**Statement:** The label's limitations of use state that the product is not indicated for
the treatment of hypoactive sexual desire disorder in postmenopausal women, is not indicated
for that condition in men, and is not indicated to enhance sexual performance.
**Date checked:** 2026-07-19.
**Source:** https://dailymed.nlm.nih.gov/dailymed/fda/fdaDrugXsl.cfm?setid=8c9607a2-5b57-4a59-b159-cf196deebdd9
**Verification:** VERIFIED 2026-07-19.
**Note:** This is the regulatory statement that most directly contradicts how the compound
is commonly marketed. It should appear alongside any mention of the approval.

### US-4. Contraindication recorded on the approved label

**Jurisdiction:** United States, FDA.
**Statement:** The approved label lists a contraindication in people with uncontrolled
hypertension or known cardiovascular disease. This is the label's only listed
contraindication.
**Date checked:** 2026-07-19.
**Source:** https://dailymed.nlm.nih.gov/dailymed/fda/fdaDrugXsl.cfm?setid=8c9607a2-5b57-4a59-b159-cf196deebdd9
**Verification:** VERIFIED 2026-07-19.
**Note:** This is a regulatory statement with practical weight, because a contraindication
only functions where a prescriber is screening for it.

### US-5. Status of material sold as PT-141 by unregulated sellers

**Jurisdiction:** United States, FDA.
**Statement:** The only FDA-approved bremelanotide product is the one described above.
Material sold as PT-141 by peptide or research-chemical sellers is therefore not an
FDA-approved product and has not been evaluated by FDA for safety, effectiveness, identity,
purity, or manufacturing quality.
**Date checked:** 2026-07-19.
**Source:** https://dailymed.nlm.nih.gov/dailymed/fda/fdaDrugXsl.cfm?setid=8c9607a2-5b57-4a59-b159-cf196deebdd9
**Verification:** PARTIALLY VERIFIED. The existence and scope of the single approval is
verified from the label. The characterisation of unapproved material is a structural
inference from that fact, not a retrieved FDA enforcement or policy document. State it as a
statement about what has not been reviewed, not as a statement about what any specific
product contains.
