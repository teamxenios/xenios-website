## 4. Disqualified source class

Vendor, reseller, marketplace, telehealth clinic, longevity clinic, affiliate, and forum
content was encountered in search results and is disqualified as evidence for every claim in
this Guide. Where such material makes a claim that conflicts with the retrieved literature,
the conflict is recorded in CONTRADICTIONS.md. Those pages are not listed individually here,
because listing them risks their being read later as a citation set.
