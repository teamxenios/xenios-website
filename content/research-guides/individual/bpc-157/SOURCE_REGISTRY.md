# BPC-157 Source Registry

this table are the ids referenced by CLAIM_TABLE.md, CONTRADICTIONS.md and
REGULATORY_STATUS.md.

"VERIFIED 2026-07-19" means the document was retrieved directly at the URL shown, on that
date. "UNVERIFIED" means the item is described only inside another source, or rests on
background knowledge, and requires a human source check.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## Source types disqualified as evidence

Vendor, retailer, marketplace, compounding pharmacy and peptide industry pages were not
used to support any claim in this Guide, including their certificates of analysis and
their summaries of the literature. Forum, social media and affiliate content was not used.
Search engine and AI-generated summaries were not used as sources. See SOURCE_PLAN.md
section 5.
