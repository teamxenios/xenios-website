# Source registry: GHK-Cu (copper tripeptide-1)

Every source consulted for this Guide, including the ones that failed to retrieve. Retrieval
status is recorded honestly. A source marked UNVERIFIED may not support a member-facing claim
until a human reviewer confirms it.

Verification key:

- VERIFIED 2026-07-19: the source was retrieved this session at the URL shown, and the content
  used from it was actually read.
- UNVERIFIED: the source was not retrieved, was retrieved only at bibliographic or title level,
  or is a tertiary or secondary summary standing in for a primary document.
