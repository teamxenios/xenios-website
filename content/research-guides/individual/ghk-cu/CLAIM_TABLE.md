# Claim table: GHK-Cu (copper tripeptide-1)

This is the controlling artifact for the GHK-Cu Guide. A statement may appear on a
member-facing surface only if it appears here and is marked allowed. Claims are graded
individually. The compound as a whole is never graded.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

Source IDs refer to SOURCE_REGISTRY.md.

## Grade key

| Grade | Meaning |
|---|---|
| A | Established |
| B | Supported human evidence |
| C | Early human evidence |
| D | Preclinical |
| E | Manufacturer or supplier reported |
| F | Traditional or historical |
| G | Unverified |
| PROHIBITED | May not appear on any member-facing surface |

Grading was deliberately conservative. Where a claim rests on a study that was uncontrolled,
unblinded, sponsored by the product manufacturer, or that tested a multi-ingredient product,
it cannot rise above E regardless of the reported result.
