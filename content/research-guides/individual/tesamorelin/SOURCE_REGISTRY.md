# Source Registry: Tesamorelin

Every source consulted for this Guide, with its retrieval status. Source ids (S1, S2 and so
on) are referenced throughout CLAIM_TABLE.md, CONTRADICTIONS.md and REGULATORY_STATUS.md.

Retrieval status is binary and literal. **VERIFIED 2026-07-19** means the exact URL below was
fetched during this session and its content was read. **UNVERIFIED** means it was not, for
any reason, and nothing sourced to it may appear on a member-facing surface without a human
first retrieving it.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## 3. Source types consulted and rejected as evidence

Encountered repeatedly during searching, particularly on the query for non-HIV human body
composition evidence, and excluded by the rules in SOURCE_PLAN.md section 5. They are logged
here for transparency, not cited.

| Source type | Why excluded | Where it surfaced |
|---|---|---|
| Peptide vendor and reseller product pages | Commercial interest in the conclusion | Repeatedly, on body composition queries |
| Telehealth and clinic marketing pages | A clinician byline on a page selling the compound does not convert marketing into evidence | Repeatedly, on body composition queries |
| Wellness and longevity institute blogs | No primary data, claims not traceable to retrievable trials | Repeatedly, on body composition queries |
| Secondary summaries of FDA and WADA documents | The primary document exists, so a summary is not an acceptable substitute. Where the primary could not be retrieved, the claim is marked unverified rather than sourced to the summary | Anti-doping status, enforcement claims, approval date |

Every result returned for the specific query on non-HIV human body composition evidence fell
into one of these excluded categories. No primary trial was returned. That absence is
reported as a finding in CLAIM_TABLE.md and CONTRADICTIONS.md rather than being filled with
excluded material.

## 4. Retrieval depth note

All five human evidence records (S5 to S9) were read at PubMed abstract level. Those abstract
records were directly fetched and verified. No full text was retrieved this session. Effect
figures and quoted phrases used in this Guide come from those abstracts. A reviewer extending
any claim beyond abstract-level detail, particularly on adverse event rates or on the
mechanics of the durability finding in S6, must obtain the full texts first.

No preclinical primary studies were retrieved. The preclinical evidence table is empty by
decision rather than by omission.
