## United States

### US-1. Approval status

**Jurisdiction:** United States, Food and Drug Administration.

**Statement:** No FDA-approved LL-37 drug product for any indication was identified in this research
session. No approval and no new drug application or biologics license application record was located
through retrieved sources. A targeted site search of fda.gov returned no FDA approval record for
LL-37, and the only FDA-domain result was an advisory committee page reference.

**How to characterize this:** as a documented negative search result, not as an affirmative FDA
statement that LL-37 is unapproved. The absence of a retrieved approval is consistent with unapproved
status. A reviewer wanting an affirmative statement must obtain it from an FDA source directly.

### US-2. Compounding status and advisory committee review

**Jurisdiction:** United States, Food and Drug Administration, Pharmacy Compounding Advisory
Committee and the Section 503A bulk drug substances list.

**Statement:** Secondary legal and trade sources report that cathelicidin (LL-37) is slated for
review by the FDA Pharmacy Compounding Advisory Committee for possible inclusion on the Section 503A
Bulk Drug Substances List, at a meeting reported as scheduled before the end of February 2027,
alongside several other peptide substances. Separately, LL-37 was not among the substances discussed
at the committee's July 2026 meeting, whose agenda covered a different set of peptides.

**Confirmation status: UNCONFIRMED.** This could not be verified from any FDA-direct or Federal
Register page. The FDA 2027 meeting materials page returned HTTP 404, the FDA Section 503A bulk drug
substances page returned HTTP 404, the FDA page on certain bulk drug substances that may present
significant safety risks returned HTTP 404, and the Federal Register notice returned a 302 redirect
to a block page. The scheduling claim therefore rests only on secondary legal and trade commentary
and vendor trackers.

**Required action before publication:** a human reviewer must check the FDA advisory committee
calendar directly and either confirm the item with a working FDA URL and a fresh date, or remove the
statement. It may not appear on a member-facing surface in its current state.

**Date checked:** 2026-07-19.

**Source URLs attempted:**
- https://www.fda.gov/advisory-committees/pharmacy-compounding-advisory-committee/2027-meeting-materials-pharmacy-compounding-advisory-committee (HTTP 404 on 2026-07-19)

## Clinical trial registration status (context, not regulatory approval)

Trial registration is not approval and does not indicate any regulatory endorsement. It is recorded
here only because it is frequently misrepresented as evidence of official standing.

A systematic sweep of ClinicalTrials.gov returned 50 records for the term LL-37. Only two of those
records administer LL-37 itself as an intervention. The remainder are observational biomarker studies
or trials of other agents (notably vitamin D and probiotics) that use LL-37 as a measured readout.

- Registry sweep, VERIFIED 2026-07-19:
  https://clinicaltrials.gov/api/v2/studies?query.term=LL-37&pageSize=50&fields=NCTId,BriefTitle,OverallStatus,Phase,Condition,InterventionName,EnrollmentCount,LeadSponsorName,HasResults,StartDate,CompletionDate
- Interventional record with posted results, VERIFIED 2026-07-19 (enrollment, disposition, and
  summary safety only): https://clinicaltrials.gov/api/v2/studies/NCT02225366
- A second interventional registration (NCT04098562, listed enrollment 40, status Unknown) was
  identified within the sweep. Its linkage to any published report was NOT confirmed and must not be
  asserted.

## Standing rules for regulatory statements in this Guide

1. Every regulatory statement carries a jurisdiction, a date checked, and a source URL. A statement
   missing any of the three does not go on a member-facing surface.
2. A failed retrieval is recorded as a failed retrieval. It is never converted into a conclusion in
   either direction.
3. Vendor, retailer, affiliate, and compounding pharmacy pages are not acceptable sources for any
   regulatory statement, including statements that appear favourable, unfavourable, or neutral.
4. Approval, registration, listing, or scheduling in one jurisdiction says nothing about any other
   jurisdiction, and silence about a jurisdiction is silence, not permission.
5. Nothing in this file is legal or regulatory advice, and nothing in it should be read as guidance
   about obtaining, possessing, or using the compound.
