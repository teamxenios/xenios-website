# LL-37 Claim Table

The controlling artifact for this Guide. Every member-facing sentence about LL-37 must trace to a
row here. If a statement is not in this table, it does not go on a member-facing surface.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## Grading key

| Grade | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| A | Established |
| B | Supported human evidence |
| C | Early human evidence |
| D | Preclinical |
| E | Manufacturer or supplier reported |
| F | Traditional or historical |
| G | Unverified |
| PROHIBITED | May not appear on any member-facing surface |

Source IDs refer to SOURCE_REGISTRY.md. Grading is deliberately conservative. Where a human trial
reported a negative or null result, the negative result is graded on the strength of the trial that
produced it, which is why several of the higher-graded rows in this table are statements that
LL-37 did not do something.

These may never appear on any member-facing surface for LL-37, in any wording, in any channel,
regardless of how a source phrases them. This section is the most operationally important part of
this table.

| ID | Prohibited claim | Why it is prohibited |
| --- | --- | --- |
| P-01 | Any amount, strength, concentration, frequency, schedule, cycle length, titration, reconstitution, preparation, or administration technique. | Categorically excluded from Xenios Research Guides. Where a reader would expect it, write: Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides. |
| P-02 | That LL-37 boosts, supports, strengthens, or enhances the immune system in humans. | No retrieved human trial studied immune outcomes in this framing, and none enrolled healthy adults. The immunomodulatory description is preclinical (C-021). |
| P-03 | That LL-37 fights, clears, kills, or protects against infection in humans. | The one retrieved human trial that measured bacterial colonization found no significant reduction versus placebo (C-017). Mechanism did not translate. |
| P-04 | That LL-37 heals wounds, or is proven, shown, or demonstrated to accelerate healing. | The largest and most rigorous human trial did not meet its primary wound closure endpoint (C-011). Outcome-guaranteeing verbs are prohibited in all cases. |

## Reviewer sign-off checklist

Before this Guide moves out of draft, a human reviewer must confirm each of the following.

1. Every member-facing sentence maps to a claim ID in Sections 1 to 6.
2. No sentence anywhere restates a Section 7 prohibition in softened language.
3. C-011 appears wherever C-012 appears. C-014 appears wherever C-013 appears. C-035 appears
   wherever C-034 appears.
4. Every grade D row states its model system in the same sentence as the finding.
5. C-051 and C-052 remain suppressed unless the reviewer has personally confirmed them against the
   FDA advisory committee calendar and the current WADA Prohibited List, on a dated check.
6. No identifier (PMID, PMCID, DOI, NCT number, author, journal, year) appears anywhere in the Guide
   that is not present in SOURCE_REGISTRY.md as VERIFIED 2026-07-19.
