## 0. THE BLEND RULE, which this gate exists primarily to enforce

**Studying an ingredient tells you about that ingredient. It does not tell you about a mixture
containing it.** A combination is a distinct preparation with its own behaviour in the body, its
own interactions between the parts, and its own unexamined questions. Only a study of the
combination itself can say anything about the combination. Where no such study exists, the
correct statement is that there is no evidence, which is a different thing from a little
evidence.

For this preparation, no combination study was located. The reviewer's central task is to
confirm that no sentence, table, heading, ordering, or juxtaposition anywhere in this folder
allows a member to come away believing otherwise.

## Standing rules for this gate

1. **No single person may sign more than one review role.** Five roles means five distinct named
   people. Combining roles defeats the purpose of the gate, which is independent checking rather
   than one person's judgment applied five times. If a role cannot be staffed, the Guide does not
   publish.
2. **AI drafts never publish automatically.** This document set was drafted by an AI system from
   three research records. It carries no editorial authority and is not member-facing until human
   sign-off is complete.
3. **An unchecked box blocks publication.** There is no override and no expedited path.
4. **A fabricated or unverifiable citation is the most serious possible failure**, because a
   reviewer or a member may trust it. If a citation cannot be confirmed at source, it is removed
   and logged as a gap. Omitting is always better than guessing.
5. **Concluding that combination evidence is absent is a successful outcome**, not a failure of
   the research. An honest empty evidence table is the correct result here and must not be
   padded, softened, or compensated for with component material.
6. **The blend rule is not satisfied by a disclaimer.** A single caveat sentence does not offset
   a document whose structure implies combination support. The claims reviewer must read each
   file straight through as a member would and judge the impression it leaves, not only the
   accuracy of its sentences.
7. **Composition must be confirmed before any product-specific statement is made.** Until then,
   this folder describes what is known about three compounds and explicitly does not describe the
   product.
8. **Regulatory status expires.** Any Guide published with a regulatory date more than 90 days old
   returns to this gate. Two of the three components are at live points in a moving regulatory
   process, which makes this folder specifically time-sensitive.
9. **Material changes restart the relevant section.** Any edit to an evidence, safety, or
   regulatory statement after sign-off requires that section to be re-checked and re-signed.
10. **If a combination study is ever located, this entire gate restarts.** The folder would then
    be describing something with evidence, which is a different document, not an amended one.
