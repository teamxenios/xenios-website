# Blend Source Registry: Thymosin Alpha-1 + KPV + LL-37

Every source consulted for this blend Guide, with its retrieval status and what it can and
cannot support. Source IDs are prefixed by component so that no entry can be quietly borrowed
across compounds.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## The first thing to read in this registry

**There is no source in this registry that studied the combination.** Every entry below tested
a single compound, or is a regulatory or supply-channel record about a single compound. No
source supports any statement about Thymosin Alpha-1 together with KPV together with LL-37, or
about any two of them together.

A registry entry supports only what that source studied, in the population it studied, by the
route it studied. Nothing in this list may be combined with anything else in this list to
produce a statement about the blend.

## Verification key

- **VERIFIED**: retrieved and read in a component research session at the URL shown, with the
  date given.
- **UNVERIFIED**: the fetch failed, was blocked, returned unparseable content, or was surfaced
  by title or metadata only. Existence may be known. Content was not read. Cannot carry a claim.
- **NON-EVIDENCE**: retrieved, but disqualified as a source of fact by the exclusion rules in
  SOURCE_PLAN.md. Listed only to record where an unusable claim originated.
