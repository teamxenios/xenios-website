# CJC-1295 and Ipamorelin Blend: Source Registry

Every source consulted for this blend Guide, with its retrieval status carried through unchanged
from the component research records of 2026-07-19. Sources marked UNVERIFIED were not successfully
read at the primary source and cannot carry a claim on their own.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## The most important row in this registry is the one that does not exist

There is no source in this registry that studied CJC-1295 and ipamorelin together. Not one, in any
species, of any design. The combination table below is empty and that empty table is the finding.

## Verification key

- **VERIFIED 2026-07-19**: retrieved and read at the URL shown during component research.
- **VERIFIED, TITLE LEVEL ONLY**: existence, identifier, title, journal, and year confirmed from a
  database listing. Content not read. Cannot carry a finding.
- **UNVERIFIED**: fetch failed, was blocked, or the claim rests on a secondary account of a primary
  document that could not be reached. Existence may be known. Content was not confirmed at source.
- **SECONDARY**: retrieved and read, but it is a report about a primary document rather than the
  document. Attributed as such everywhere it is used.
- **NON-EVIDENCE**: disqualified as a source of fact by the exclusion rules in SOURCE_PLAN.md.

Reviewer note: these entries are stamped 2026-07-19 to match the component research retrieval date.
The files in this folder carry a review date of 2026-07-21. Reconcile before publication.
