# BPC-157 + TB-500 + GHK-Cu Blend Source Registry

Every source consulted for this blend Guide, with its retrieval status. Sources marked
UNVERIFIED were not successfully read and cannot carry a claim on their own. Sources marked
NON-EVIDENCE exist only to record where unusable material originated.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## Verification key

- **VERIFIED 2026-07-19**: retrieved and read in the underlying research session at the URL
  shown.
- **VERIFIED 2026-07-19 (title only)**: the bibliographic record was retrieved, the abstract
  was not. No claim rests on the findings of such a record.
- **UNVERIFIED**: the fetch failed, was blocked, or returned unparseable content. Existence may
  be known from metadata, but the content was not read.
- **NON-EVIDENCE**: retrieved, but disqualified as a source of fact by the exclusion rules in
  SOURCE_PLAN.md.

Note for the reviewer: the underlying research records carry retrieval dates of 2026-07-19,
while these files carry a review date of 2026-07-21. Reconcile before publication, and re-date
every regulatory statement to the actual human verification date.
