# Semax + Selank + DSIP Source Registry

Every source behind this Guide, with its retrieval status carried through honestly from the
underlying research records. Sources marked UNVERIFIED were not read in full and cannot carry
a claim on their own. Sources marked NON-EVIDENCE exist only to record where an unusable
claim originated.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## Verification key

- **VERIFIED 2026-07-21**: retrieved and read at the URL shown, and recorded as `verified: true`
  in the underlying research record.
- **UNVERIFIED**: recorded as `verified: false` in the underlying research record. The fetch
  failed, was blocked, returned empty content, or the statement rests on a tertiary source or a
  search summary rather than the primary document. Existence may be known. Content was not
  confirmed.
- **NOT RETRIEVED**: the fetch failed outright and no content from it is asserted anywhere.
- **NON-EVIDENCE**: retrieved, but disqualified as a source of fact under SOURCE_PLAN.md
  section 5.

A note the reviewer should read first: the balance of this table is unusual. Of the regulatory
sources, exactly one primary document was successfully retrieved. Everything else in the
regulatory sections is unverified or secondary. That is a weakness in the chain, and it is
recorded here rather than smoothed over.
