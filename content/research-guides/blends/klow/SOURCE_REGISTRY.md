# KLOW Source Registry

Every source consulted for the KLOW Guide, with its retrieval status and what it is permitted
to support. Sources marked UNVERIFIED were not successfully read and cannot carry a claim on
their own.

Dosing and administration information is intentionally excluded from Xenios Research Guides.

## Two standing warnings about this registry

**First: not one source in this registry studied the KLOW combination.** Every entry below is
about a single compound, a single bulk substance, a supply channel, or a regulatory process.
No entry may be used to support a statement about what the blend does, and no group of entries
may be assembled into one.

**Second: no source in this registry establishes what KLOW contains.** S00 records the origin
of the reported ingredient list and is explicitly classified as non-evidence. There is no
supplier document in this registry, because none was available.

## Verification key

- **VERIFIED 2026-07-21**: retrieved and read at the URL shown in the supplied research record.
- **VERIFIED (titles only)**: bibliographic metadata confirmed, individual findings not read.
- **UNVERIFIED**: fetch failed, was blocked, or returned unparseable content. Existence may be
  known from metadata, but the content was not read.
- **NON-EVIDENCE**: recorded to document the origin of an unusable claim. Carries nothing.

Note for the reviewer: entries are stamped 2026-07-21 to match the review date on this file.
The underlying research records carry retrieval timestamps of 2026-07-19 and 2026-07-20.
Reconcile before publication.
