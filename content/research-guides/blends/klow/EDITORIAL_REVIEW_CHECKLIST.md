## 0. The two failures that would matter most on this product

This section exists because KLOW is a blend whose composition is undocumented. These are the
two errors most likely to occur and most damaging if they do. A reviewer should check this
section first and, if either fails, stop rather than continue down the list.

**The blend rule, stated for this gate:** a mixture is a distinct pharmacological object with
its own interactions and its own unknowns. Evidence gathered on a compound studied by itself
tells you nothing about a preparation in which that compound sits alongside others. Only a
study of the actual combination can produce evidence about the combination. Where no such study
exists, the correct statement is that there is no evidence, not that the evidence is limited.

## Standing rules for this gate

1. **Studying the ingredients is not studying the mixture.** A combination is its own
   pharmacological object with its own interaction profile and its own unknowns. Where no study
   administered the combination, the honest statement is that there is no combination evidence,
   not that it is limited or emerging. This rule governs every other rule below and is the
   reason this gate exists in this form.
2. **An unconfirmed composition is a publication blocker in its own right.** Until a supplier
   specification exists, this Guide may describe only what is reported and by whom. No
   composition, strength, or ratio may be published as fact, and no visual or editorial
   treatment may imply one.
3. **AI drafts never publish automatically.** This document set was drafted by an artificial
   intelligence system from four supplied research records. It carries no editorial authority.
4. **An unchecked box blocks publication.** There is no override and no expedited path.
5. **No reviewer signs twice.** Five distinct named individuals, one role each.
6. **A fabricated or unverifiable citation is the most serious possible failure**, because a
   reviewer or a member may trust it. If a citation cannot be confirmed at source, it is removed
   and logged as a gap. Omitting is always better than guessing.
7. **Concluding that combination evidence is absent is a successful outcome**, not a failure of
   the research. An honest empty evidence table is the correct result here and must not be
   padded with component findings.
8. **Regulatory status expires.** Any publication carrying a regulatory date more than 90 days
   old returns to this gate. The pending advisory committee review makes this product
   specifically time-sensitive.
9. **Material changes restart the relevant section.** Any edit to an evidence, safety,
   composition, or regulatory statement after sign-off requires that section to be re-checked
   and re-signed.
10. **If a study of this combination appears, the entire evidence structure changes.** This
    document set returns to the gate in full rather than being amended in place.
