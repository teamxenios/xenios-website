# Momentous supplement candidate records (14 to 25)

## Scope and standing conditions

This file is an internal candidate registry. It is not a catalog, not an offer, and not a
member-facing surface. Every record below is authorization pending.

Standing conditions that apply to all 12 records without exception:

- Reseller authorization state: NOT AUTHORIZED. Xenios holds no written reseller,
  distribution, or affiliate agreement with Momentous. Nothing here may state or imply that
  Xenios Research sells, stocks, or is authorized to resell any Momentous product.
- MAP state: NOT CONFIRMED - see open supplier questions. Pricing, margin, minimum
  advertised price policy, and inventory are all NOT CONFIRMED.
- Commerce state: BLOCKED pending written authorization.
- Catalog state is separate from commerce state. These records may exist while commerce
  stays disabled.
- Guide state: no member guide drafted for any record. Guides are member only. Guide
  completion is not a commerce prerequisite.
- Publication: AI drafts never publish automatically. Samuel Boadu is founder reviewer and
  publisher.

## Dosing exclusion notice

Dosing information is intentionally excluded from every record. Amounts, strengths,
frequency, timing, cycle length, titration, loading, and stacking instructions are omitted
by policy even where the official page publishes them. Some official product titles include
a strength descriptor. Where that is the case, the descriptor is stripped and the fact is
noted in the record.

## Verification method

Each record was verified by retrieving the official Momentous product page on 2026-07-19.
Ingredients, allergen statements, and certification claims are recorded only where they
appeared on that retrieved page. Where a certification claim appears in secondary Momentous
marketing pages but conflicts with the product page, the conflict is flagged for human
confirmation rather than resolved.

Claim evidence grades used below: A established, B supported human evidence, C early human
evidence, D preclinical, E manufacturer or supplier reported, F traditional or historical,
G unverified, PROHIBITED not permitted on any member-facing surface.

No human clinical literature was retrieved this session. Goal mappings are therefore
recorded as proposed routing hypotheses at grade G unless the line is explicitly graded
otherwise, and every ingredient function statement is either grade E (published by the
manufacturer) or labeled as background knowledge requiring a human source check.

without a named human reviewer clearing them:

- L-Glutamine and Recovery (both supply glutamine)
