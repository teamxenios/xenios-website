This file is an internal candidate registry only. It is not a catalog, not a price list, and not

- Commerce state: BLOCKED pending written authorization.

  published. AI drafts never publish automatically. Samuel Boadu is founder reviewer and publisher.
- Catalog state is separate from commerce state. A record may exist here while commerce stays disabled.

Fulfillment note carried from the founder canon: for the first roughly 60 days, Mitch owns peptides
and Quantum, and Xenios owns supplements. Peptide commerce is not authorized. Quantum commerce is

## Content safety rules applied to this file

- Dosing information is intentionally excluded. No amounts, strengths, frequency, timing, cycle
  length, loading, or stacking instructions appear anywhere in this file, including where an official
  page published them and including where a brand product title contains a strength designation.
- No treatment directions, protocols, or regimens appear here.
- No guaranteed outcomes. No general safety claims. No statement that any product is safe, well
  tolerated, or free of side effects.
- Goal mappings below are internal catalog routing labels. They are not efficacy claims, not medical
  advice, and not a statement that any product produces any outcome.
- Ingredient and certification entries reproduce official label and page data only, described in
  plain terms. No brand marketing copy is reproduced.

## Evidence grading used in this file

A Established. B Supported human evidence. C Early human evidence. D Preclinical. E Manufacturer or
supplier reported. F Traditional or historical. G Unverified. PROHIBITED means the claim may not be
made on any member-facing surface.

Composition, format, and certification entries in this file are grade E, because the single retrieved
source for each is the brand's own product page. No efficacy claim in this file is graded above G,
because no human clinical source was retrieved in this session. Any future upgrade above G requires a
retrieved primary human study attached to the specific claim.
