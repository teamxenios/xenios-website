## Dosing exclusion notice

Dosing information is intentionally excluded from every record. Amounts, strengths, colony
forming unit counts, frequency, timing, cycle length, titration, loading, and stacking
instructions are omitted by policy even where the official page publishes them.

Some official product titles include a strength descriptor. Following the convention set in
the Momentous records, the descriptor is stripped from the recorded product name and the
fact is noted in the record. Record 42 is affected. It was requested under a working label
that included a strength descriptor, and that descriptor is stripped here rather than
reproduced.

One exception is made, deliberately and narrowly. The official URL for Record 42 contains
the strength in its slug. The URL is recorded verbatim because a URL is a verification
identifier, not a dosing instruction, and altering it would break the ability to check the
record. No strength is written into any product name, prose line, or field elsewhere.

## Verification method and its limits (read before using this file)

This is the important caveat for this file, and it differs from the Momentous records.

**No official Pure Encapsulations product page was directly retrieved this session.** Every
WebFetch request to `pureencapsulationspro.com` returned HTTP 403 Forbidden, including
requests to product pages and to the brand's own PDF product information sheets. The brand
domain refused automated retrieval.

Verification here is therefore a weaker tier, and records are graded accordingly:

- **URL CONFIRMED (counted as verified, 6 records).** An official product URL on the
  `pureencapsulationspro.com` domain was returned by WebSearch this session. The URL's
  existence on the brand domain is confirmed by the retrieved search index result. The page
  content was NOT retrieved or read.
- **UNVERIFIED (4 records).** No official product URL was returned for the requested item
  before the session web search budget was exhausted. No URL is recorded for these. No URL
  has been guessed, patterned, or reconstructed.

Consequences that must not be glossed over:

1. **No ingredient list in this file is confirmed against an official label.** Constituent
   names appearing below come from search result summaries that may blend official and
   retailer sources. Every one is marked indicative and requires confirmation against the
   official label before any member-facing use.
2. **No certification claim is verified for any record.** NSF Certified for Sport, Informed
   Sport, and equivalent third party sport certifications were NOT verified on any official
   page for any of the 10 records. No such claim may be repeated anywhere.
3. **No allergen statement is confirmed for any record.** Allergen fields read NOT CONFIRMED.
4. A secondary site, `purecapspro.com`, was fetchable and returned a page for the Record 37
   product. Its provenance as a brand-operated property is NOT CONFIRMED, so it is treated as

Re-verification by a human, loading each official page in a normal browser, is required
before any record here advances. That re-verification is the gating task for this file.

Claim evidence grades used below: A established, B supported human evidence, C early human

No human clinical literature was retrieved this session. No PMID, NCT number, DOI, author,
journal, or study year is cited anywhere in this file, because none was retrieved. Goal

Date note: this registry carries the assigned review date of 2026-07-19. Retrieval was

## Duplication rules applied across this file

The registry duplication rules are applied as follows within this set:

- Records 36, 37, and 38 are all gut microbiome products. **No more than one may be routed to
  a member at a time without human review.** Records 36 and 38 are both multi strain
  probiotics and must never be routed together.
- Records 42 and 43 are **overlapping sleep formulas**. Record 43 is indicated to contain
  melatonin. Routing 42 and 43 together would stack melatonin from two sources. **Blocked by
  rule.** Never both.
- Record 38 is indicated to contain L-Glutamine, which duplicates Record 39. Do not route
  both without human review.
- Records 44 and 45 are both stress and adaptogen products with probable ashwagandha overlap.
  Do not route both without human review.
- Record 41 contributes zinc, copper, manganese, folate, and biotin. It must be checked
  against any multivitamin or standalone zinc product elsewhere in the registry before
  routing. Two multivitamins, two zinc products, two magnesium products, two omega-3
  products, and multiple high dose vitamin D products remain prohibited combinations
  registry wide.
