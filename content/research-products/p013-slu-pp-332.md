## 2. Canonical name

SLU-PP-332 Capsules

Member-facing brand form: Xenios Research. Internal prose uses lowercase xenios.

The compound referred to in the retrieved literature as SLU-PP-332 is a small synthetic
molecule described as a pan agonist of the estrogen-related receptors (ERR alpha, beta and
gamma). It is not a peptide, despite being sold as one by parts of the online research
chemical market. Any member-facing surface that calls this a peptide is inaccurate and must
be corrected.

## 14. Prohibited claims

  body composition promises, percentage fat loss figures presented as expected outcomes, or

  benefit claim for human beings. The retrieved findings on glucose tolerance and fat mass are

- Any longevity, lifespan, healthspan, or anti-aging claim.
- Any cognitive enhancement claim.
- Any sexual performance or vitality claim.

Therapeutic, curative and clinical framing:

## 18. Product FAQ

Written for a member. No dosing, no promises.

**What is SLU-PP-332?**
It is a synthetic small molecule that researchers designed to activate a family of proteins
called the estrogen-related receptors. It is often grouped with peptides by online sellers,
but it is not a peptide.

**What has it actually been studied for?**
In the published work we retrieved, it has been studied in mice for effects on fat oxidation,
fat mass, glucose tolerance and energy expenditure, and in cultured cells and laboratory
systems for effects on genes involved in oxidative metabolism. Those are animal and laboratory
findings.

**Has it been tested in people?**
We did not find any human clinical trial of SLU-PP-332. That means the human effects, the
human safety profile, and the human dose response are unknown. We are not going to fill that
gap with an assumption.

**Why does the record say so much is not confirmed?**
Because we have not received or verified a certificate of analysis, a composition
specification, or storage and handling data from a supplier. We would rather publish an honest
record with gaps in it than a confident record we cannot stand behind.

**Can I buy this from Xenios Research?**
No. Commerce for this lane is not authorized and is blocked pending written approval. The
catalog record exists so that the documentation work can happen in the open. It is not a
storefront.

**Why is there no dosing information anywhere in this record?**
Dosing information is intentionally excluded. Publishing amounts or schedules for a compound
with no human trial data would be presenting instructions we have no basis to give, and we do
not do that.
