# Research Products

page is later built from. They are not marketing pages and they are not sales copy.


## Commerce reality, stated plainly

Peptide commerce is **not authorized**. Quantum commerce is **not authorized**. Supplement
reseller activity is **not authorized**. Every record in this tree carries a commerce approval
state of blocked or pending written approval.

Catalog state is separate from commerce state. A record exists here so the catalog can be
designed and reviewed. Existence of a record is not permission to sell.

## What is deliberately absent

No record states composition ratios, concentration, fill volume, shelf life, storage
temperature, sterility, purity, COA content, supplier identity, price, margin, or inventory.
None of that is confirmed. Every such field reads `NOT CONFIRMED - see open supplier
questions`, and the corresponding question is recorded in the record and in
`docs/research-content/OPEN_RESEARCH_GAPS.md`.

Inventing any of those values would be worse than leaving them blank, because a later reader
could act on them.

## Supplement candidates

Every supplement record is a **candidate only**. Xenios holds no written reseller authorization
from Momentous, Pure Encapsulations, or any other brand. Reseller authorization state is
`NOT AUTHORIZED` on every record without exception, and commerce state is `BLOCKED`.

Nothing in that folder may be presented as a product Xenios sells, stocks, or is authorized to
resell.

See `docs/research-content/PRODUCT_CONTENT_SCHEMA.md` for the field contract.
